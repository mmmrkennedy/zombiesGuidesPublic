Liberty Falls Guide

Jet Gun (Thrustodyne M23)

The Jet Gun is available from the mystery box, through the completion of S.A.M. trials, and via the following quest:

  1. Walk up to this door on the upper level of the Motel. It will fly open and Zombies will spill out. Inside is the workbench used to build the Jet Gun.
  2. The Jet Gun requires three parts to be built - Gauge, Handbrake, and Wires:
    • Gauge
      1. Pick up the Water Valve from the water pipe, right of Speed Cola on Washington Ave.
      2. Go to the Bowling Alley and shoot this panel to knock it off the wall. Interact with the gauge to place the valve.
      3. You must now hold interact on the valve to "build pressure". While turning the valve, Zombies will start spawning.
        • You can stop interacting with the valve if needed, but you can’t leave the Bowling Alley for too long. if you leave, your progress will reset, but you can retry immediately.
      4. Once enough pressure is built, the Zombies will die and the gauge will fall to the floor for you to pick up.
    • Handbrake
      1. Wait in the Cemetery until a Zombie spawns called the "Groundskeeper." Kill it and pick up the Toolshed Key he drops.
        • This Zombie is not aggressive and will not attack you. He will spawn in, wander around the Cemetery, and teleport away.
        • There is no known way to spawn the Groundskeeper (if you even can spawn him manually), so just spend a couple of rounds in the Cemetery, and he should spawn.
      2. Once you have the key, interact with the door of the shed in the Groundskeeper’s Yard. The door will open, allowing you to pick up the Handbrake on the table inside.
    • Wires
      1. Obtain a Mangler Cannon or Mutant Injection scorestreak.
      2. In the Riverside area, shoot the metal gate blocking the entrance to the Radio House store with a Mangler Cannon (either by you or an enemy Mangler). This will cause the gate to break open.
      3. On the floor inside the store, there are four garbage piles. Interact with each of the piles until the Electrical Wires spawn. Pick up the wires.
  3. Once you have all three parts, the Jet Gun can be built on the workbench in the Motel.

'Bye-Bye, Dark Aether' Main Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 2/10, Est. Completion Time - 30 to 45 Mins

    Building the L.T.G.

  1. Interact with this machine in the Church (called the SDG). All Zombies will die and an in-game story cutscene will start.
  2. Obtain the Jet Gun.
  3. There are three parts to the L.T.G. around the map that you must suck up using the Jet Gun’s primary fire (the suck). Part locations:
    • Dark Aether (Church) - Floating on the left side of the upper level in the Church
    • Olly’s Comics - In the ceiling above the desk in the middle of the room
    • Washington Ave. - In a window above Speed Cola. Stand on the truck to see it.
  4. Once you have all three parts, the L.T.G. can be crafted at the workbench on "The Alamo" (the roof of the Bank).

    5. Canister #1

  5. Interact with the machine in the Church (called the SDG) to pick up an Aether Canister.
  6. Go to 'Hill Street' and drop the canister on one of the Dark Aether Field Generator nodes (the thing that produces the field).
  7. Go to the 'Cemetery' and place the L.T.G. on the metal under the Aether cloud storm. This will start a timed event where you must defend the L.T.G. from Zombies.
    • If you fail to defend the L.T.G., you will have to wait until the next round to retry.
  8. Once you've defended the L.T.G., it will turn red and spawn an HVT. Shoot the HVT until it glows purple (happens at about 10% health remaining).
  9. Once the HVT is glowing purple, bring it to the trap where you dropped the canister and activate it. Kill the HVT while it is standing in the trap. If done correctly, the canister will suck up the "essence" of the HVT. The HVT and canister have to be in the same trap "node".
    • If you kill the HVT early, end the round and interact with the L.T.G. to respawn the HVT.
  10. Pick up the canister and bring it back to the SDG before the countdown expires.
    • If you don’t bring it back in time, wait one round then restart from Step 8.

    Aether Projectors

  11. Pick up a Strauss Counter from the front-left side of the SDG. It may take a couple of seconds to spawn due to a conversation.
  12. There are three Aether Projectors around the map that need to be set to the correct colour using the Strauss Counter (see below). When near a projector, pull out the counter and look at the colour it is displaying. Based on this colour, you will need to set the projector to a different colour by interacting with it to change the light on the top. Projector locations:
    • Groundskeeper’s Yard - Right of the stairs down to the street
    • Hilltop Stairs - On the grass beside the stairs
    • Yummy Freeze Rooftop - Drop down from the door, left of the arsenal machine on "The Alamo" roof
    Strauss Counter Colour Colour on Projector
    Red Green
    Green Red
    Yellow Yellow
  13. Once all three are set correctly, the projectors will move to face spawn and shoot a beam of energy. Once all three are set correctly, the projectors will move to face spawn and shoot a beam of energy. After a few seconds, the canister will come out of the top of this device on 'East Main Street'. Interact with it to pick up the canister.

    14. Canister #2

  14. Drop the canister on one of the Dark Aether Field Generator nodes in spawn.
  15. Place the L.T.G. under the Aether Storm in the 'Riverside' area. (if you can’t, make sure you picked it back up from the Cemetery). You will have to repeat steps 7-10 but with the new Aether Storm, canister, and Aether Trap.
  16. Once the canister is brought back to the SDG, you will be able to interact with it to enter the boss fight.

    17. Boss Fight

  17. All players will be teleported to the 'Dark Aether' where you must survive three waves of enemies.
  18. On wave 1, kill all the Zombies to progress. After the first wave, a Full Armour power-up will spawn.
  19. For waves 2 and 3, HVTs (one per wave on Solo, more per wave on Co-op) will spawn; Zombies will start infinitely spawning and will only stop when the HVT(s) are killed. After wave 2, a Max Ammo will spawn.
  20. Once the final wave is complete, all players will be downed and the final cutscene will begin. After the cutscene ends, you can choose to keep playing. If you do, all players will be given all perks, as well as a bunch of loot.

Mister Peek Bowling Mini-game (Free Loot/Gun and Dark Ops Calling Card)

  1. There are five pairs of Bowling Shoes you must shoot. Shoe locations:
    • Pump & Pay - On the cashier desk, inside a barrier
    • Riverside - On a shelf inside the store right of the Arsenal machine
    • Hill Street - Under a chair inside the house up the stairs from the AK-74 wallbuy
    • Cemetery - Hanging from a tree outside the barrier, right of the Armour wallbuy
    • West Main Street - Inside the funeral home, on top of the casket
  2. Once all five shoes are shot, all players will be teleported to the Bowling Alley.
  3. To play, you will need to pick up a Bowling Ball from a Ball Return machine and throw it at as many Zombies as you can. Hitting a Zombie will award you with 10 points. Charging up the throw (by holding down the button) will allow you to hit multiple Zombies with one throw.
    • Each player has their coloured ball and ball return machine. So don’t try and use anyone’s ball and stay near your ball return. Ball colour is indicated by the colour on your HUD.
    • When charging up a throw, ignore the HUD arrow indicator, as this doesn’t indicate throw power. Instead, wait until the ball starts CHANGE ME.
  4. Rewards will be awarded CHANGE ME. If any player gets at least 300 points, all players will be awarded the Dark Ops Calling Card "Strike!".
  5. You can replay the Mini-game an additional CHANGE ME number of times per game by shooting the pair of shoes in the toilet in the Bowling Alley.

"Destroy Something Beautiful" Song

  1. Interact with three "Mister Peeks Headphones" around the map. Headphone locations:
    • Motor Lodge Alley - On the ground in front of the crafting table
    • Dark Aether - On one of the pews
    • Washington Ave. - On the ground beneath a bench left of the Gobblegum machine
  2. Once all three headphones are interacted with, the song will start.

Opening the Bank Vault (Free Loot)

  1. There are three numbers on sticky notes around the map. Each sticky note will indicate the order the number is in by having underscores in place of the other numbers. Example: "__ __ 26", this means 26 is the last number in the order. Sticky Note locations:
    • Liberty Lanes - On the concession counter. Shoot the object covering the note to reveal it.
    • Olly's Comics - Under and to the left of the cash register
    • Savings & Loan - On top of the main reception desk, on the side closest to the Vault
  2. Once you have the three numbers, interact with the Bank Vault to enter the numbers. If the numbers were entered correctly, you will now be able to enter the vault. On your first entrance, you will be awarded with 500 points.
  3. You can now use the Loot Keys dropped by killing enemies on the safety deposit boxes inside the vault.

Mr. Peeks Car (Free Gun)

  • Around the map, there are three black and yellow cars. One of the cars will have Mister Peeks in the back seat. Destroying the car with a Mangler Cannon shot (either by you or an enemy Mangler) will cause a free gun to come out of the trunk. Car locations:
    • Hilltop - Right of the Gobblegum machine
    • Groundkeeper's Yard - Near the Crafting Table
    • Backstreet Parking - Left of the Crafting Table near Olly's Comics
  • After about five rounds, Mister Peeks will respawn in one of the other cars (assuming it is not already destroyed), allowing you to do this again.

Vending Machine (Free Loot/Weapon)

  • Melee the Vending Machine below the stairs of the Motel to receive a random piece of loot. This can only be done once per round per player. You must crouch and melee the part of the vending machine where you would collect your item (when used in real life).
  • If a player melees the machine with the Melee Macchiato perk equipped, the machine will spit out a bunch of loot and break. Once the machine is broken, you can no longer get loot for the remainder of the game.

Play as the Aetherella Trap

  1. Obtain the Jet Gun.
  2. Using the Jet Gun’s primary fire (the suck), you must suck up nine Aetherella dolls. When a doll is collected, a sound will play. Doll locations:
    • Inside Olly's Comics - On the table right of the door to the parking lot
    • Inside Olly's Comics - On a shelf on the wall, left of the Aetherella trap
    • Inside Olly's Comics - On the wall by the window, right of the door near the Armour wallbuy
    • Inside Olly's Comics - On a shelf, left of Quick Revive
    • Motel - On the Motel sign on the right side of the building
    • Washington Ave. Rooftops - On the A.C. unit across the street (when looking North)
    • Forecourt - On the front-right windowsill of the Church
    • West Main Street - On the Liberty Lanes sigh. You need to be standing on the bus
    • Yummy Freeze Rooftop (drop down from "The Alamo") - On a nearby rooftop, between some HVAC units
  3. Once you collect all the dolls, you will immediately turn into Aetherella. This will last for 2-3 minutes. While playing as Aetherella, you will become invincible to all damage, and shoot laser beams that will auto-target nearby enemies (specials and elites too). Buying the Aetherella trap while playing as Aetherella will give you increased movement speed.

Deadshot Sharpshooter (Free Deadshot Perk)

  1. Obtain a Sniper or a weapon with high zoom scope (this is not required but it makes it a lot easier). The LR 7.62 can be purchased from the wallbuy on Washington Ave. Rooftops.
  2. In the Cemetery outside the map, right of the Ammo Box, there will be five Deadshot Daquiri cans on a fence.
  3. You must shoot the cans without missing them or taking too long.
    • If you miss a can or take too long, all the remaining cans will disappear, and you can retry in two rounds.
  4. Once you shoot all five cans, you will be awarded with Deadshot Daquiri. This will be lost when you go down (like any other perk).

Raining Zombies (Free Loot)

  1. On the top-left of the church, you must explode a head using a lethal or explosive weapon.
  2. Once it is destroyed, Zombies will start raining from the sky around the Church Forecourt and parking lot. When they hit the ground, they will drop points, salvage, or other rewards such as aether tools.

Red Orb (Free Loot/Fire Sale Power-up)

  1. Throw three semtex grenades in the center of the Bloody Swimming Pool behind the Motel.
    • If you take too long to throw the Semtex grenades, you can retry CHANGE ME.
  2. If done fast enough, after a couple seconds, a red orb will fly out of the swimming pool and drop Salvage and Equipment.
  3. This can be completed two more times, every five rounds. On the third completion, the orb will drop a fire sale power-up along with the other loot.

Free Power-ups

  • There are seven power-ups hidden around the map, that, when shot, will spawn that power-up. Each power-up can be obtained once per match. Locations:
    • Max Ammo: Abandoned Rooftop/East Main Street - On the right side of the bridge, at the base of the yellow support
    • Bonus Points: Riverside - On the backside of Olly’s Comics, on an A.C. unit in a window
    • Max Armour: Washington Ave. - On a metal cart in the barrier across the street from the Ammo Box
    • Double Points: Groundskeeper’s Yard - On one of the supports of the water tower
    • Insta-Kill: Cemetery - On the roof of the Church at the back (can be seen by standing near the Armour wallbuy
    • Nuke: "The Alamo" - Looking through the skylight farthest from the Arsenal, in the chandelier in the Bank
    • Full Power: West Main Street - Behind the flaming barrier near the bus

Dancing Zombies

  1. In the Riverside area, use the two Binoculars to view the dancing Zombie on the cliff across the river.
  2. Once both Binoculars are looking at the Zombie, two big laser beams will appear near the Zombie. Also, the bus near the binoculars will begin playing music, and all the Zombies on the map will run to the bus and start dancing.
    • The dancing will last for CHANGE ME.

Pool Table (Free 100 Points)

  • Shoot the Cue Ball (white ball) on the Pool Table in Liberty Lanes. If done correctly, every ball will fall into a pocket and 100 points will spawn.
