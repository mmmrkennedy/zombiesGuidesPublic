'MEAT IS MURDER' Main Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 3/10, Est. Completion Time - 30 to 45 Mins, RNG Rating - 3/5 (Magnetron in the Box)

    Seasoning the Pan

  1. Obtain the Exo Suit in the Sewers.
  2. After obtaining an Exo Suit, return to Spawn. Climb up to the highest crate and look for a golden frying pan on the cracked rooftop. Use an Exo Boost jump to reach and grab the Golden Frying Pan.
  3. Head back down to the Sewers and look for four orange valves on four connected pipes. These valves will spawn randomly on any of the following locations. Once you find a valve, interact with it to let out some steam.
    • Video of all 32 Locations (YouTube)
  4. Once you turn all four valves, a secret passage will open behind the waterfall in the Sewers.
  5. Go through the secret passage where you will find an altar. Interact with the altar to place the golden frying pan and lower the altar. You now need to kill a certain number of Zombies around the altar (only in the lower part of the room) to fill the pan with blood.
  6. When enough zombies are killed, the altar will rise, and you can pick up the pan.
  7. Take the pan to Burger Town and place it on the stoves in the kitchen.

    8. Making the Burger

  9. With all four pieces of meat on the stove, you will need to cook them. Obtain the Magnetron from the 3D Printer and blast the meat until you hear a ding. The meat should resemble a burger patty.
  10. Now you will need to find a bun. To obtain a bun, you will have to kill a Zombie wearing a Burger Town uniform, which is usually found around the Burger Town area. Kill one of these Zombies and pick up the bun they drop by walking over it. With the bun, interact with the meat patty on the stove to create a burger.
  11. Interact with the completed burger to pick it up. You will immediately become infected. Run down (or take the sewer chute on the side of the Burger Town) to the Decontamination Zone in the Sewers to disinfect yourself and cleanse the burger.
  12. Return to Burger Town and interact with Bubby to feed him the burger. He will tell you his batteries are low.

    13. Charging Bubby

  13. Head over to the Command Center in Atlas Command. You should see a screen with a schematic of Bubby inside this mobile building. Interact with the screen and then quickly run to the other Command Center where the power generator is located. A small box should open up with a battery inside. If you don't get it in time, you will have to interact with the Bubby screen again.
  14. Take the battery over to a charging station in Value Voltage. Place the battery in the charging station to begin charging it. While the battery is powering up, Zombies will be attracted to it and will try to knock it out of the charging station. If this happens, you will have to pick it back up and place it back in the station.
  15. Once the battery is filled, it will turn red, and you can pick it up. With the battery, head back to Burger Town and give Bubby the Battery.

    16. Launching the Rocket

  16. Bubby will present you a key; interact with it to pick it up. This key opens up the hatch on the large burger on top of the Burger Town building. Climb up the ladder and use the key to enter the burger. Interact with the Bubby schematic screen in the burger, and you'll get an "Access Denied" screen.
  17. Head down into the Sewers and activate the Insta-Gator Trap. Wait for the Alligator to snatch up a Zombie. While the gator is out of the water, shoot it as fast as possible to blow it up. It should drop a Zombie arm, floating and spinning in the water for you to pick up.
  18. Take the Zombie arm to the fingerprint scanner upstairs in the Spawn room. This will open up a safe with a red electronic tablet for you to pick up.
  19. Take the tablet into the large burger and interact with the Access Denied screen again. You will get kicked out of the burger, and several bombs will shoot out of the tower. After a few seconds, a rocket will shoot out of the burger tower.
    • Players can pick up these bombs by walking over them. They can then be thrown at zombies to damage/kill them.
    • The Bombs CAN damage you when they blow up, so watch out for them. If not picked up in time, the bombs will blow up.
  20. Climb back into the large burger and grab the blue tablet from underneath the Bubby screen. Give this tablet to Bubby to complete the Easter Egg.
  21. As a bonus, Bubby will grab a sign and start walking around, attacking the zombies. After one full round, he will explode, leaving behind four power-ups.

'Toccata and Fugue in D minor' song

Link to song on YouTube.

  1. Three Golden Rockets need to be interacted with to start the song. Interacting with a rocket will play a brief section of the song. Rocket Locations:
    • Value Voltage - On a shelf facing the Upgrade Station
    • Atlas Command - On the ground, to the right of the Exo Slam machine
    • Burger Town - On the rooftop, in the opening in the fence near the big burger sign
  2. Once all three rockets are interacted with, the song will start.
