'Game Over, Man!' Main Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 1/10, Est. Completion Time - 20 to 30 Mins, RNG Rating - 2/5 (EM1 from Box)

    The Black Box

  1. Obtain the Black Box found on the ground in Spawn left of the crashed Warbird.
  2. Navigate to the Morgue and interact with the Computer Terminal on the right side of the room to place the Black Box. Once complete, a quote from Angie will play.

    3. Steps 3, 4-7, 8-10, and 11-12 can be completed in any order

    Lilith's Keycard

  3. Acquire the EM1 from the 3D Printer. Next, while shooting the 3D Printer with the EM1, purchase it again. The player (Lilith, if available) can now obtain the Access Card from the 3D Printer, incrementing their Clearance Level by 1 (indicated by number on the keycard on the bottom left of the HUD ). If no one in the lobby is Lilith, then anyone can collect the Access Card.

    4. Decker's Keycard

  4. RECOMMENDED: Keep the EM1 (if Lilith's Card was obtained before this) as you can fire at the ground and mash interact without having to reload.
  5. In Solo, purchase a Trash Chute and DO NOT enter (by backing away from it); instead wait for it to close, then buy it again immediately after and enter. In Co-Op, two players must purchase and enter two different Trash Chutes at the same time.
  6. The player(s) will be taken to the Incinerator, where you need to shoot the floor until an Access Card is revealed. The card will fly up into the air when the right area on the floor is shot.
  7. The player (Decker, if available) can now obtain the Access Card from the ground, incrementing their Clearance Level by 1 (indicated by number on the keycard on the bottom left of the HUD ). If no one in the lobby is Decker, then anyone can collect the Access Card.

    8. Oz's Keycard

  8. Navigate to the Holding Room, shoot the metal bar beneath the middle holding cell which reacts when shot. Continue to shoot it until it disappears.
  9. All players must now buy Exo Slam, then return to the Holding Room and simultaneously slam on the top of the outermost holding cell. If done correctly, the holding cell will collapse while another raises.
    • After a couple seconds, the Holding Cell that came up after the slam, will lower back down. If this happens before the player(s) pick up Oz's Card, you must slam the Holding Cell again.
  10. The player (Oz, if available) can now obtain the Access Card from the holding cell, incrementing their Clearance Level by 1 (indicated by number on the keycard on the bottom left of the HUD ). If no one in the lobby is Oz, then anyone can collect the Access Card.

    11. Kahn's Keycard

  11. During an Infected round, navigate to the Administration area and get infected. There are four keypads you'll need to interact with (in any order) that can only be used while infected.
    • The Keypads are NOT timed, but all four must be interacted with in one "instance" of being infected. If you cure yourself, you will have to interact with each keypad again.
    • Keypad locations:
      • On the security checkpoint door across from Exo Medic
      • Across from the entrance door next to the Decontamination Shower
      • At the end of the red hallway around the right corner inside Administration
      • On a second floor balcony, left of the MP11 wallbuy
  12. After using all four keypads, return to the security checkpoint and the window will open up revealing the Access Card. The player (Kahn, if available) can now obtain the Access Card from the window, incrementing their Clearance Level by 1 (indicated by number on the keycard on the bottom left of the HUD ). If no one in the lobby is Kahn, then anyone can collect the Access Card.

    13. Getting Clearance Level 49

  13. Killing Zombies will now randomly drop Access Cards which can be obtained by simply walking over the drop location, incrementing their Clearance Level by 1. Collect cards until every player in the lobby has a Clearance Level of 49.
  14. Navigate to the Morgue and interact with the terminal where the Black Box was placed.
  15. Each player must locate and interact with their designated Access card Reader. Once complete, the player's Clearance Level will be increased to 50.
    • Lilith: Morgue - On the second floor in the room between the Exo Reload room and Exo Training Room
    • Decker: Exo Training Room - Halfway up the stairwell
    • Oz: Holding - Left of Exo Health
    • Khan: Administration - Left of the door leading to the Morgue, across from the MP11 wallbuy
  16. Interact with the Computer Terminal in the Morgue again. Angie will say, "Authorization Successful..."
  17. All players must now simultaneously interact with the Exo Suit Station. Once complete, Angie will begin to speak, and you will now need to navigate to Spawn to witness the Outro. Also, the Experimental Weapon Upgrade Station in the Exo Testing Room will unlock which can be used to upgrade a weapon from Mk 20 to Mk 25.

'Game Over, Man!' Gold Statue / Alternative Ending

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 3/10, Est. Completion Time - 25 to 35 Mins, RNG Rating - 2/5 (EM1 from Box)

  1. None of the players in the match can go down; doing so will void completion, and you will get the normal ending if you continue the quest. Also, this ending will automatically end the game.
  2. Complete the Main Quest like normal up to step 12.
  3. On step 13, you must kill the Zombies only using melee attacks. They will still drop the Access Cards, but your Clearance Level number on the HUD will stay light grey.
    • If you collect a card from a Zombie that wasn't killed by melee, the number on the HUD will turn dark grey, and you will have to restart the game to try again.
  4. Once you get a Clearance Level of 49 and the number is still light grey, you can continue the steps like normal.

'Ride of the Valkyries' Song

Link to song on streaming here.

  1. Three tools need to be interacted with to start the song. Interacting with a tool will play a brief section of the song. Tools Locations:
    • Administration - Wrench sticking out of the wall right of the Weapon Upgrade Station
    • Exo Testing Room - Bonesaw on a table behind two big doors right of the trash chute
    • Holding - On the table with the corpse, left of the weapon upgrade station
  2. Once all three tools are interacted with, the song will start.
