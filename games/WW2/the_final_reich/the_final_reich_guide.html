Power

  1. Activate three valves near the 'Village Square' by interacting with them until the dial turns green and hits 60. Releasing interact early resets the valve to 0. Follow the tubes from the "generator" left of the Armour station to find and track the valves' status. Valve locations:
    • Village Square - Behind the Armour station
    • Village Square - Left of the Bunker entrance
    • Riverside - Near the entrance to the 'Pub'
  2. Once the three valves are activated, interact with the Pilot Light in the middle of the ‘Village Square’. This will cause the fire to come shooting out of the hole along with some Pest Zombies.
  3. Drop into the hole and make your way up the ramp to the generator at the top. Interact with it to activate Power.
  4. Open the door to the Bunker then to the ‘Morgue’ and ‘Laboratory’. Once both doors are opened, you need to redirect power from each area by interacting with the power switch in each area within about a minute.
    • If you take too long, the activated switch will deactivate and need to be activated again.
  5. Once both switches are activated, the door to the Salt Mine Tunnels can be opened for 1500 Jolts.

Pack-a-Punch

  1. Activate the Power
  2. Obtain the Spectral Shield
  3. Spirit blast the Voltmeter on the roof to spawn the Pack-a-Punch.
  4. The Pack-a-Punch will teleport between the following three locations. There is no way (that we know of) to figure out where the Pack-a-Punch is without checking each location. Locations:
    • Roof
    • Power House
    • Building 64

CHANGE ME

The CHANGE ME can be Pack-a-Punched and does not require an Easter Egg to do so.
