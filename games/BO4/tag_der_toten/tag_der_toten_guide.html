Power

There are a total of three Power Switches in the map, each activating power for its corresponding area.

  • Docks
  • Bridge
  • Human Infusion (Zipline Handle required)

Pack-a-Punch

  1. Activate Power at the Docks and Bridge.
  2. In the 'Lighthouse Approach' cave, shoot/melee the Blue Rock (Location 1, Location 2 or Location 3) to make it fall to the ground for you to pick up.
  3. Navigate to 'Lighthouse Level 4' and place the rock on the Pulley that the Hermit lowers down.
  4. Occasionally, the lighthouse will shine its light on a location in the map. This will cause the Pack-a-Punch machine to appear there for a limited amount of time (~5 Mins).

    Golden Pack-a-Punch

    Golden Pack-a-Punch will immediately upgrade a weapon to its highest level, instead of requiring four uses to upgrade damage like with normal PAP.

    1. Activate Pack-a-Punch and obtain the Zipline Handle.
    2. Activate Power Switch in 'Human Infusion'.
    3. Occasionally, the lighthouse will not shine at one of the normal locations, but instead shine at an iceberg off the side of the ship. This is also indicated by a sound cue, different from sound when the PAP is at a new location.
    4. All players must stand on the Flinger on the 'Sun Deck' of the ship to be flung to the iceberg. While on the iceberg, the Golden Pack-a-Punch will be able to be used for 5000 points (or 2500/1500 Points on an already PAPed weapon) and Zombies will start spawning infinitely. After about two minutes, all players will be flung back to the ship.

Flinger Repairs

  • Around the map, there are two flingers which need to have their gearboxes repaired by the Hermit.
  • Pick up a gearbox and give it to the Hermit; he will repair it and return it. Return the gearbox to the Flinger to activate it.
    • Navigation
    • Facility (You will need a Dynamite Bomb to clear the debris)

Challenge Totems

Around the map, there are five Challenge Totems, each with three challenges. To activate the Challenge Totems, you must go to 'Lighthouse Level 4' and wait for your character to talk to the Hermit.

  1. Frozen Crevasse
    • Kill enemies while standing in freezing water (Reward: Bonus Points Powerup)
    • Shatter frozen zombies (Reward: Max Ammo Powerup)
      • Zombies can be frozen by either killing them with snowballs or shooting, but not killing them while they are standing in water.
    • Play the Hermit's favourite tune on the bells in 'Docks' (Reward: Increased snowball storage, from 6 to 10)
      • The Hermit will "sing" five notes in a random order.
      • You must play these notes by shooting (or throwing snowballs at) bells corresponding to a note in the order given.
        • C Bell - On the side of the Boathouse, right of the Essex Model 07 wallbuy
        • A Bell - On the middle dock, beside the power switch
        • G Bell - On the elevated area at the back where you spawned
        • E Bell - Just outside the map, left of the Strife wallbuy
  2. Lighthouse Station
    • Kill enemies with headshots and collect brains (Reward: Carpenter Powerup)
    • Kill enemies with a Shield bash (Reward: Full Power Powerup)
    • Pick up Pee Jars in 'Lighthouse Level 4' and empty them in the Beach Water (Reward: A Self Revive)
      • Sprinting or leaving the ground for ANY reason while carrying a jar will result in the jar breaking, making it, so you'd have to pick up a new one.
  3. Beach
    • Kill enemies with snowballs (Reward: Bonus Points)
    • Kill enemies using the Lighthouse trap (Reward: Insta-kill)
    • Complete 3 SOS messages with snowballs (Reward: Break Ice faster)
      • Around the map, there are three incomplete SOS messages painted on walls. You will need to fill the holes in the letters by throwing snowballs at them. Message Locations:
        • Beach - On the side of the boat behind the Beach Challenge Totem
        • Lighthouse Station - On the side of the Lighthouse, just below the Level 4 catwalk
        • Sunken Path (To access, either shoot this pipe in 'Artifact Storage' or place a Dynamite Bomb on this debris in the 'Lagoon') - On the side of the Boat
  4. Forecastle
    • Kill enemies while standing still (Reward: Carpenter Powerup)
    • Kill enemies with melee attacks (Reward: Fire Sale Powerup)
    • Find soup ingredients, add them to the cooking pot, and add water (Reward: Armour Powerup)
    • Ingredient Locations:
      • Location 1:
        • Gangway - On the Red Booth Seat
        • Gangway - On the island counter-top
        • Gangway - On the bar counter-top
      • Location 2:
        • Beach - On the wooden barrel, right of the 'Beach' Challenge Totem
        • Beach - In a bush, near the water
        • Beach - In the wheelbarrow, left of the M1927 wallbuy
      • Location 3:
        • Boathouse - On a Crab trap, right of the MOG 12 wallbuy
        • Boathouse - On the radiator, beside the doorway to the Lighthouse
        • Boathouse - On the stove, left of the Zombie spawn
    • Once you have all three soup ingredients, place them in the Pot above the Campfire on the 'Forecastle', then throw a snowball into the Pot.
  5. Facility (Zipline Handle Required)
    • Fling Zombies, See Flinger Repairs for how to repair the flingers (Reward: Insta-kill)
    • Kill zombies while riding a zipline (Reward: Team Full Power)
    • Complete the Wack-a-mole station using only snowballs (Reward: Traps are half-price)
      • The Wack-a-Mole minigame can be found on the 'Loading Platform' of the Facility.
      • Wack-a-mole consists of three rounds, where you must throw snowballs at puppets are they appear out of pipes.
      • To start round 1, throw the snowball at the target behind the pipes.
      • Round 1 consists of 5 targets, Round 2 consists of 10 targets, and Round 3 has 15.
      • A new round will start automatically after one is completed.
      • If you fail a round, throw a snowball at the target to restart that round.

Thundergun

The Thundergun is a wonder weapon that fires blasts of incredibly strong compressed air, with the ability to kill an entire horde in one shot. The Thundergun can be Pack-a-Punched and does not require an Easter Egg to do so.

The Thundergun can be obtained via the Mystery Box or the following quest:

  1. Complete all five Hermit Challenge Totems.
  2. Navigate to 'Lighthouse Level 4' and collect the Key from the Hermit's pulley.
  3. Interact with the Reward Crate in 'Lighthouse Level 4' to open it, revealing a free Thundergun.

Tundragun

The Tundragun is a heavily modified Thundergun that fires cryo rounds. The Tundragun does heavy splash damage to the player making PhD Slider basically a requirement to use it. The Tundragun can be upgraded via the Pack-a-Punch.

The Tundragun can be obtained via the Mystery Box or the following quest:

  1. Obtain the Zipline Handle and Repair the Flinger on the Boat.
  2. You must hit five targets with snowballs in a specific order within 1 Min and 45 Secs. When you hit a target successfully, it will make a ding sound. If you take too long or hit the wrong target, you retry immediately with the first target. Target Locations and Order:
    1. Loading Platform - Above doorway to inside Facility
    2. Lagoon - Near the bottom of the Ice Slide
    3. Sun Deck - Just outside the room with the power switch
    4. Lighthouse Level 3 - On the center pole of the spiral staircase
    5. Cargo Hold - On a support beam
  3. If done correctly, the Wooden Box, left of the last target, will open to reveal the Tundragun for you to pick up.

Wunderwaffe DG-Scharfschütze

The Wunderwaffe DG-Scharfschütze is a "sniper" that fires electric round that will chain to nearby enemies. Its power is amplified when landing headshots on zombies, killing faster as well as chaining to more enemies with a single shot. The Wunderwaffe DG-Scharfschütze can be upgraded via the Pack-a-Punch.

The Wunderwaffe DG-Scharfschütze can be obtained via the Mystery Box or the following quest:

  1. Locate, shoot down and pick up an Icicle in one of the following locations:
    • Beach - On the Cave Entrance on the way to the Lighthouse
    • Lighthouse Station - On the 'Lighthouse Level 4' broken railing
    • Lighthouse Cove - On the stairs above
  2. Navigate to the 'Forecastle' and place the Icicle into the Pot above the Campfire. Once the Icicle has melted, collect the Key from the Pot.
  3. Obtain the Zipline Handle.
  4. Interact with the Safe in 'Specimen Storage' to open it, revealing the Vril Device. Kill Zombies near the Safe until the Vril Device starts to glow.
  5. Give the Vril Device to the Hermit, and in return he'll give you the Reward Crate Key.
  6. Interact with the Reward Crate in 'Lighthouse Level 4' to open it, revealing a free Wunderwaffe DG-Scharfschütze.

Riot Shield

  1. The Riot Shield requires three parts to be built:
    • Part 1:
      • Docks - Leaning against the boat, left of the power switch
      • Docks - Leaning on the gas pump, on the dock on the far side of the area
      • Docks - On a snowbank, near the door to the 'Boathouse'
    • Part 2:
      • Frozen Crevasse - On the elevated area on the right when coming from the 'Docks'
      • Frozen Crevasse - Left of the Mystery Box location
      • Lagoon - Right of the Pack-a-Punch iceberg
    • Part 3:
      • Lighthouse Level 1 - Left of the doorway to 'Lighthouse Cove'
      • Lighthouse Level 2 - Between the "2" painted on the wall and a Zombie spawn
      • Lighthouse Level 3 - Just below the stairs up to 'Lighthouse Level 4'
  2. Craft the Riot Shield in the Lighthouse Station or Specimen Storage.

Samantha's Music Box

  1. Obtain the Zipline Handle and activate all three Power Switches.
  2. Navigate to the Facility and find two key cards in the following locations:
    • Key Card 1:
      • Decontamination - In the dryer at the bottom of the stairs coming from the 'Security Lobby'
      • Decontamination - In one of the eye flush stations in front of the Tonic Perk Machine
      • Decontamination - In the sink, left of the Tonic Perk Machine
    • Key Card 2:
      • Specimen Storage - In the Mail Boxes, behind the desk with the Gramophone
      • Specimen Storage - In the toilet of the left-most cell
      • Specimen Storage - On the bottom shelf of the canister rack, right of the buildable table
  3. Interact with the Security Panel in the 'Security Lobby' to insert the Key Cards. Wait a few seconds, then pick the Key Cards back up.
  4. In the 'Human Infusion' area, insert the Key Cards into the card readers on both sides of the Vault Door.
  5. Interact with the Vault Door to start a lockdown. Once the vault door is fully opened, Samantha's Music Box can be obtained from the vault box found within. Once you pick up the Music Box, the lockdown will end.
    • Note: Samantha's Music Box will replace the current lethal equipment (Exp. Wraith Fire, Monkey Bomb, etc.) Snowballs can be picked up while you have the Music Box and will not replace it, just like when you have other equipment.

Dynamite Bomb

  1. Wait for a Flaming Zombie to spawn.
    • Note: Flaming Zombies only spawn when you DON'T have a crafted Dynamite Bomb or all three required parts in your inventory.
  2. Kill it with either Snowballs (Snowballs are always a one-shot kill headshot) or the Tundragun to make it drop a part.
  3. Repeat two more times for two more parts.
  4. Once you have all three parts, the Dynamite Bomb can only be crafted in the Gangway.

Heat Pack

  1. Obtain the Zipline Handle and a Dynamite Bomb, and activate all three Power Switches.
  2. Make sure the 'Frozen Crevasse', 'Lagoon', and 'Lighthouse Cove' areas are opened.
  3. Repair the Flinger on the 'Outer Walkway' of the Facility.
  4. Based on where you stand on the Flinger (left, middle, right), you will be flung to one of three locations. You will need to be flung to each of these locations as when you land, you will land on a box, breaking it, revealing a part. Box locations:
    • Left - Frozen Crevasse
    • Middle - Lagoon
    • Right - Lighthouse Cove
  5. Craft the Heat Pack in the Lighthouse Station or Specimen Storage.

Zipline Handle

  1. Locate a Zipline Crank, which can be found on the 'Stern' in one of the following locations:
    • Stern - Inside the enclosed area, left of the Zombie spawn
    • Stern - Outside the enclosed area, left of the Soda Perk Machine
    • Stern - Leaning against the side of the ship, right of the Bowie Knife
  2. Place the Zipline Crank on the Zipline Box on the 'Sun Deck' and interact with it until the crank stops spinning. This will cause the Zipline from the 'Sun Deck' to the 'Beach' to be moved into position.
  3. Locate another Zipline Crank, which can be found on the 'Forecastle' in one of the following locations:
    • Forecastle - Left of the Mystery Box
    • Forecastle - Left of the Auger DMG wallbuy
    • Forecastle - Right of a Zombie spawn, near the zipline to 'Lighthouse Level 4'
  4. Place the Zipline Crank on the Zipline Box on the 'Main Deck' and interact with it until the crank stops spinning. This will cause the Zipline from the 'Lighthouse Level 4' to 'Cargo Hold' to be moved into position.
  5. Once both Ziplines are moved into place, return to the Hermit, where he will present you with the Zipline Handle.

'Salvation Lies Above' Main Quest

  1. Example

'A Light from the Shore' Song

  • Interact with three melee weapons found around the map, locations below:
    • Sickle: Ice Grotto - At the bottom of the Ice Slide
    • Pitchfork: Loading Platform - On the snowbank, in the middle of the area, near the Zipline
    • Machete: Hidden Path - On a wooden fence post, right of the Maddox RFB wallbuy
  • Once all three are interacted with, the song will play.

'Reflections' Song

  1. Interact with the large, metal door on the bottom floor of the 'Lighthouse Station' three times. Each time should be accompanied by a knocking sound.
  2. Once complete, the song will begin to play.

Yellow Snowballs

  1. Throw snowballs at four sock puppets around the map. Locations:
    • Samantha's Sock Puppet
      • Unlock Sunken Path by either shooting this pipe in 'Artifact Storage' or place a Dynamite Bomb on this debris in the 'Lagoon'.
      • Sunken Path - On a rock, behind the GKS wallbuy
      • Sunken Path - On the broken boat, left of the GKS wallbuy
      • Sunken Path - Left of the previous location, on top of a big rock
    • Eddie's Sock Puppet
      • Lighthouse Level 2 - On a fence post, outside the window facing the Ship
      • Lighthouse Level 3 - On a power line, outside the window closest to the door to the 'Lighthouse Station' stairs
      • Lighthouse Level 4 - On top of a wall light, above the spiral staircase
    • Margwa Sock Puppet
      • Geological Processing - On top of the machine in the middle of the room
      • Geological Processing - On top of a shelf to the left, when coming form 'Security Lobby'
      • Geological Processing - At the back of the room, on a power box, inside a Zombie spawn
    • Fluffy's Sock Puppet
      • Sun Deck - On the end of a smoke stack, toward the 'Stern' of the Ship
      • Sun Deck - Above the Zombie spawn, beside the stairs
      • Sun Deck - On top of the zipline
  2. Around the map, there will be three Campfires, one of which will be glowing blue. Kill Zombies near the blue Campfire to charge it. Once charged, it will go out and one of the remaining Campfires will glow blue. Campfire locations:
    • Beach - Near the Beach Challenge Totem
    • Boathouse - Between the Pack-a-Punch location and the actual Boathouse building
    • Frozen Crevasse - Left of the Mystery Box, on the elevated platform (Zombies have to be killed on the platform to count)
  3. In 'Human Infusion', the tank above you will be cracked. Shoot it to cause the Spleen inside to fall to the floor.
  4. Once you've picked up the spleen, a ticking sound will begin playing that will slowly get faster. The faster it gets, the less time you have. Make your way to the 'Loading Platform' as quickly as you can and take the Zipline to the 'Docks'. Once there, jump in the water. This will cause the timer to start ticking slower. Stay in the water till the cooldown on the zipline to the 'Lighthouse Level 4' is over, after which you should take the zipline to the lighthouse. Give the Spleen to the Hermit.
    • If you take too long to deliver the Spleen, you can pick it up again from the floor of 'Human Transfusion' immediately.
  5. Once you give the Spleen to the Hermit, he will give you Yellow Snowballs. Once picked up, all snowballs around the map will be replaced by a pile of Yellow Snowballs.

Free Perk

  1. Unlock Golden Pack-a-Punch
  2. A miniature figurine can be found around each of the Pack-a-Punch spawn locations, only when the ice is melted at that location. Figurine Locations:
    • Iceberg (Golden Pack-a-Punch) - Right of Machine
    • Boathouse - Right of Machine
    • Beach - Behind Machine
    • Sunken Path - Behind Machine
    • Lagoon - Behind Machine
  3. Interact with the suitcase on the 'Beach' to place the miniatures. This will cause a wave of Electric Zombies to spawn next round.
  4. Once the wave is complete, a Free Perk powerup can be found in the suitcase.

Margwa Jumpscare

  • Aim at this paper in the 'Forecastle'.

The Hermit Jumpscare

  1. Obtain a scoped weapon (the Wunderwaffe DG-Scharfschütze does work for this). The Koshka wallbuy can be found in the 'Security lobby'.
  2. Navigate to the 'Facility Entrance' and aim at the Light from the Lighthouse.

George Romero Tribute / Free 500 Points

  • Hold interact on the Pair of Glasses on the cabinet by the stairs in the 'Lighthouse Station'. Your character should say something about them, and you will be given 500 points.

'The Secret' Easter Egg/Achievement

  1. Choose either option (the first option is recommended): Equip the 'Talkin' Bout Regeneration' Elixir AND/OR equip the 'Dying Wish' Perk to your loadout.
  2. Obtain the Heat Pack and complete the Beach Challenge Totem.
  3. Make sure you have Dying Wish and your Specialist Weapon charged and 'Talkin' Bout Regeneration' ready.
  4. Make your way to the Facility Flinger, crouch, then walk on to the back-left of the Flinger and spin as fast as you can, while remaining crouched (increase controller sensitivity to at least 8).
  5. If done correctly, you should be launched to a far away iceberg. Activate your elixir and start swimming. Keep following the path through the tunnels until you make it to the end. After a while, you will start taking damage (from no air).
    • If you have the elixir active, you will heal right back up.
    • If you don't have the elixir, keep swimming till Dying Wish activates, then near the end of it, pull out your specialist weapon for the extra armour.
  6. Once you make it to the end, you will exit the water and be presented with the Secret. There is no physical reward for doing this, other than an achievement. You can return to the normal map by interacting with the Dock.
