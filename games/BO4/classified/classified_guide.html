Classified Guide

Power

  • There is one power switch. It's in the room behind the Soda Perk.

Pack-a-Punch

Note: You can still enter the 'Panic Room' after placing the Teleporter Signal Amplifier by activating the Defcon switches and then entering the teleporter at the back of the 'Server Room.'

  1. Craft the Teleporter Signal Amplifier, three parts are required:
    • Teleporter Pad:
      • Main Offices - Under Desk near Teleporter
      • Main Offices - Top of the fallen desk, near the Mystery Box Location
      • Main Offices - Left of the Brew machine
    • Amplifier:
      1. Activate the four Defcon switches in any order:
        • Server Room - Left of the Room
        • War Room: Lower Level - Left of the elevator to the Labs
        • War Room: Upper Level - Right of the elevator to the Offices
        • War Room: Upper Level - Right of the Spitfire wallbuy
      2. Enter the teleporter in the 'War Room: Lower Level' to go to the 'Panic Room.'
      3. Collect the Amplifier.
    • Wires:
      • Morgue - Top of chair, right of Mystery Box Location
      • Morgue - Corner of countertop
      • Morgue - On a body chamber
  2. Craft the Teleporter Signal Amplifier at Central Filing or Weapon Testing.
  3. Place the Teleporter Signal Amplifier on the small teleporter in the 'War Room: Lower Level.'
  4. Now when the Defcon level is set to five, the big teleporter in the middle of the 'War Room: Lower Level' will teleport the player to 'Groom Lake' where Pack-a-Punch is.

Winter's Howl (Project Skadi)

The Winter's Howl can be Pack-a-Punched and doesn’t require an Easter Egg to do so.

The Winter's Howl is available through the mystery box and via the following quest:

  1. Recommended: Equip a Frag Grenade and complete the Nova Crawler Easter Egg on Round 1 to get 6000 points for free.
  2. To obtain the Winter's Howl, you need to get four sets of numbers which correspond to four previous maps. These numbers can be obtained in any order.

    3. Der Riese Numbers

  3. Pick up the Key in the box opposite the Cola Perk, in the 'War Room: Lower Level.'
  4. Navigate to the 'Main Offices' and interact with the desk in the center of the room to open the drawer to reveal a picture of Der Riese with numbers on it. Note the numbers.

    5. Shangri-La Numbers

  5. Activate Power.
  6. If you don't have Frag Grenades equipped, purchase and Pack-a-Punch the M1897 Trebuchet from the wallbuy in the 'War Room: Lower Level' near the lower elevator.
  7. In the 'South Laboratories,' navigate to the barrier near 'Weapon Testing.' Throw a Grenade/Shoot a Trebuchet shot behind this machine.
  8. If done correctly, a sound will play and the Shangri-la picture will spawn on the right side of the barrier.

    9. Kino der Toten Numbers

  9. Open Pack-a-Punch
  10. Activate the Defcon Switches in the following order:
    1. War Room: Upper Level - Right of the Spitfire wallbuy
    2. Server Room - Left of the Room
    3. War Room: Upper Level - Right of the elevator to the Offices
    4. War Room: Lower Level - Left of the elevator to the Labs
  11. Enter the 'Panic Room' via the teleporter in the 'Server Room.'
  12. Interact with this TV to make it slide back, revealing the Kino der Toten numbers below where it was.

    13. Shi No Numa Numbers

  13. Obtain any Pack-a-Punched weapon (the Pack-a-Punched Welling that the player receives while down on solo works).
  14. In the 'Deserted Hallway,' there are four pictures of historical American Figures. Shoot their nameplates in the following order:
    1. George Washington (1st from left)
    2. Benjamin Franklin (3rd from left)
    3. Alexander Hamilton (2nd from left)
    4. Abraham Lincoln (4th from left)
  15. If done correctly, George Washington's nameplate will rise, revealing the Shi No Numa Numbers.

    16. Input the Numbers

  16. In the 'War Room: Lower Level,' there is a machine with flashing pictures and a number pad.
  17. Shoot the numbers to change them, then interact with the button on the right to enter the code. After each correct number is input the light above the button will light up green, and the picture of the map will appear on screen.
  18. You must enter the numbers from the pictures in the following order:
    1. Deserted Hallway Paintings (Shi No Numa)
    2. Main Offices Drawer (Der Riese)
    3. South Laboratories Barrier (Shangri-La)
    4. Panic Room TV (Kino der Toten)
  19. If you enter the wrong number, you must restart with the Shi No Numa code.
  20. If done correctly, after a few seconds, a voice and the screen on the machine will say "Project Skadi Retrieved."
  21. Teleport to 'Groom Lake' and survive three full rounds without leaving. This will open the barriers on the left of Pack-a-Punch.
  22. The Winter's Howl can be picked up from the blue glowing case.

Riot Shield

  1. The Riot Shield requires three parts to be built:
    • Part 1:
      • Central Filing - Near the filing cabinets
      • Central Filing - Near the doorway to 'Side Passage'
      • Central Filing - Left of 'Main Offices' entrance
    • Part 2:
      • War Room: Lower Level - Right of the doorway to the 'Server Room'
      • War Room: Lower Level - Below the staircase
      • War Room: Lower Level - Behind the Mystery Box location
    • Part 3:
      • South Laboratories - Left of the Power Switch
      • South Laboratories - Left of the teleporter, outside 'Weapon Testing'
      • North Laboratories - Right of barrier, near Auger DMR wallbuy
  2. Craft the Riot Shield at Central Filing or Weapon Testing.

Electric Traps

  1. There are two electric traps on the map, both require a trap part to activate. There are three locations for each part:
    • Server Room:
      • Right of the Defcon Switch
      • Near the Teleporter
      • Left of the doorway, leaving the room
    • Porcine Research:
      • Right of the barrier, left of the KN-57 wallbuy
      • Right of the KN-57 wallbuy
      • Left of the doorway leaving the room
  2. Both Traps are located in the hallways on either side of the spawn room and look like Metal Detectors. Locations:
    • Deserted Hallway
    • Side Passage

Main Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 9/10, Est Completion Time - 4 Hrs to 6 Hrs

Recommended Loadout (Anything not listed is Player's Choice)
Perks Special Weapons Elixirs Equipment

  • Secret Sauce (if you get the Hellion Salvo get PhD otherwise use Dying Wish)
  • Stonecold Stronghold
  • Timeslip (Modifier)

  • 'Temporal Gift'
  • 'Equip Mint'

  1. Unlike every other Main Quest, there's only one step: Reach Round 150. The cutscene will start upon finishing Round 149. Round 150 Strategy:
    • Build the Riot Shield in 'Weapon Testing.' You will be using the Shield, Wraith Fire and the Ragnarok DG-5 to get kills.
    • Stand in this corner next to the Shield workbench while holding your shield out.
    • Plant your Ragnarok DG-5s as soon as they’re ready. Otherwise, throw a Wraith Fire at your feet.
    • When your Wraith Fires and Ragnaroks aren’t ready, shoot the zombies with the Shield gun until a Wraith Fire or the Ragnaroks recharge.
    • Use Equip Mint whenever you have it and you run out of Wraith Fires.
    • Try to not let your Shield break, keep buying a new Shield from the bench as the current one gets low on health or ammo.
    • If you'd rather use the Hellion Salvo, swap Dying Wish for PhD Slider and shoot the Hellion at your feet until the Ragnaroks recharge. Return to using the Shield when the Hellion runs out of ammo.

'Shockwave' Song

  1. Three bottles need to be interacted with to start the song:
    • Central Filing
    • War Room: Lower Level
    • Porcine Research
  2. Once all three bottles are interacted with, the song will start.

'This Jazz is Classified' Intro Cutscene Song

  1. There are five computers in the 'War Room: Lower Level' that need to be interacted with in the following order to start the song:
    1. War Room: Lower Level - Left of the doorway to 'Server Room'
    2. War Room: Lower Level - Near elevator to Labs
    3. Server Room - Otherside of the computer, closest to the Defcon switch
    4. War Room: Lower Level - Left of ICR-7 wallbuy
    5. Server Room - In the middle of the room, facing the teleporter
  2. Once all five computers are interacted with, the song will start.

Last Gen Richtofen Jump Scare

  1. Open Pack-a-Punch
  2. Obtain a Sniper with a default scope. The Koshka can be purchased from the wall in the 'Weapon Testing' for 4500 points.
  3. Navigate to 'Groom Lake' and aim at this light outside the map, left of Pack-a-Punch.

Nova Crawler Easter Egg / Free ~6,000 Points

  • In the 'Main Offices,' interact with this door left of the Brew Perk Machine five times.
    • Interacting with the door will play a knocking sound, and Robert McNamara will speak to you.
    • Interacting with the door twice will spawn five Hellhounds, along with the knocking sound, and a Robert McNamara voice line.
    • After interacting with the door for the fifth time, Samantha will yell at you and 40 Nova Crawlers will spawn. Killing all the Nova Crawlers with melee will net you about 6000 points. The Double Points and Temporal Gift Elixirs can be used to get 12,000 points.
