Perk Statues

  • There are four Perk Statues, three of which change locations for each new game.
  • One perk will always be on the 'Mausoleum,' another in the 'Gardens' and the last will either be in the 'Wine Cellar' or 'Forest Terrace.'
  • There is a way to easily tell which Statue is at the 'Mausoleum' and 'Gardens' by checking the symbols above the doorways to the Library and Dining Room. The Library door corresponds to the Mausoleum and Dining Room door to the Gardens.
  • Doorway Symbols:
    • Danu: Always in the Entrance Hall
    • Ra: Doorway Symbol
    • Zeus: Doorway Symbol
    • Odin: Doorway Symbol

Alistair's Folly

Alistair's Folly is a pistol that fires an exploding round, which does not damage the player. It cannot be Pack-a-Punched, but there are two upgrades, the Chaos Theory and Alistair's Annihilator.

The Alistair's Folly is available through the mystery box and via the following quest:

  1. Locate four symbols, each a different colour. The possible symbols are stylized versions of "X", "A", "D" or "Z", so when you find a symbol note down the colour and letter (exp. "Yellow Z"). Symbol Locations:
    • Red Symbol:
      • Master Bedroom - Right of fireplace
      • East Gallery - At the top of the stairs to the 'Main Hall'
      • Wine Cellar - On a wooden barrel outside the barrier, right of the MX9 wallbuy
      • Dining Room - Above painting behind the bar
    • Yellow Symbol:
      • Forest Terrace - On a Wall, left of Bowie Knife wallbuy
      • Forest Terrace - Behind a gate, left of the GKS wallbuy
      • Forest Terrace - Behind the GKS wallbuy
      • Forest Terrace - Behind a gate, right of the Bowie Knife wallbuy
    • Green Symbol:
      • Greenhouse Terrace - Outside a gate, left of the entrance to the 'Greenhouse Laboratory'
      • Gardens - Inside the gazebo, left of Vapr-XKG wallbuy
      • Gardens - Outside a gate, right of Perk Statue
      • Gardens - Outside a gate, left of Perk Statue
    • Blue Symbol:
      • Cemetery - On a gravestone, behind a tree, near the Stake Buildable Table
      • Cemetery - On the gravestone, right of the right-side staircase to the 'Mausoleum'
      • Mausoleum - Top of a crypt, right of the 'Mausoleum'
      • Mausoleum - Base of lion statue, left of the 'Mausoleum'
  2. Enter the symbols into the Lock Box at the back of the Library.
  3. If the correct symbols were input, an alarm sound should play and the window will open, allowing the player to pick up Alistair's Folly.

    Chaos Theory

    The Chaos Theory is an enhanced version of Alistair's Folly that has two new attacks which can be triggered by holding down the trigger until the weapon is fully charged. The new attacks are a Tornado that picks up any Zombies that enter and a Yellow/Green Pool that turns any Zombies that enter friendly.

  1. There are two parts required to upgrade to the Chaos Theory: The Gun Part and the Werewolf Chaos Materia. These parts can be obtained in any order.

    2. Werewolf Chaos Materia

  2. Obtain Silver Bullets
  3. Kill a Werewolf with a weapon equipped with Silver Bullets. If done correctly, Werewolf Chaos Materia should drop from the Werewolf.
  4. Place the Werewolf Chaos Material into the machinery in the 'Greenhouse Laboratory.' After a few seconds, the machine will produce Prima Materia to be picked up from the left side of the machine.

    5. Gun Part

  5. Obtain the Ballistic Shield
  6. Melee the bookshelf at the back left of the 'Library,' right of the Silver Bullets buildable table, with the Ballistic Shield. The bookshelf should slide open revealing a gun part glowing blue.

    7. Crafting the Weapon

  7. Craft the Chaos Theory at the buildable table left of the stairs in the 'Greenhouse Laboratory.'

    Alistair's Annihilator

    Alistair's Annihilator is an enhanced version of the Chaos Theory that has two new charged attacks, as well as the attacks the Chaos Theory had. The new attacks are a Red Orb that vaporizes Zombies that enter and a Green Pool that shrinks Zombies to death.

  1. There are three parts required to upgrade to the Chaos Theory: The Gun Part, the Nosferatu Chaos Materia and the Bat Chaos Materia. These parts can be obtained in any order.

    2. Nosferatu Chaos Materia

  2. Use the Tornado attack from the Chaos Theory to kill vampires and make them drop Bile. Collect three Bile; after obtaining three, no more bile will drop.
  3. Interact with the red glowing coffin in the 'Cemetery' to smear the bile on the side. After a few seconds, a Crimson Nosferatu will spawn from it. Kill it to make it drop the Nosferatu Chaos Materia.

    4. Bat Chaos Materia

  4. In the 'Mausoleum,' shoot the orange lamp with the Chaos Theory to switch it off and make another orange. After the first lamp is shot, quickly shoot the next three lamps, or they'll all go out, requiring you to wait till next round to retry.
  5. Once all four lamps are shot, a bat will spawn from the lamp and fly around the 'Cemetery' and 'Mausoleum.'
    • Tip: As soon as you shoot the last lamp, aim just above the lamp to where the bat is spawning and start shooting the Chaos Theory. If done correctly, you should kill the bat before it can fly into the sky.
  6. Shoot it with the Chaos Theory to obtain the Bat Chaos Materia.

    7. Gun Part

  7. In the 'Forest,' there are three dirt piles with glowing mushrooms, with one hiding a gun part. When a zombie is near/on a pile, hit it with the Chaos Theory's green pool attack. If done right, the zombie will start digging. If that pile has the gun part, you can collect it; if not, repeat with the other piles until you find the part.

    8. Crafting the Weapon

  8. Convert the Nosferatu Chaos Materia and Bat Chaos Materia into Prima Materia via the machinery in the 'Greenhouse Laboratory.' Note: Only one Materia can be converted at once.
  9. Craft the Alistair's Annihilator at the buildable table right of the stairs, near the ICR-7 wallbuy in the 'Greenhouse Laboratory.'

Savage Impaler

The Savage Impaler is a crossbow that does heavy damage to vampires, often one-hit killing the regular ones. It cannot be Pack-a-Punched, and there is no upgrade for it.

The Savage Impaler can be obtained from the following quest or the mystery box after quest completion:

  1. Around the Mansion, six burning candles need to be meleed to be put out (the Strife's knife attachment does work). When meleeing the candle, aim for the base of the flame as the hitbox is small. Each time a candle is put out, a sound will play. Locations:
    • Master Bedroom
    • East Gallery
    • Music Room
    • East Hallway
    • Wine Cellar
    • Study
  2. In the 'Forest,' to the right of the Pack-a-Punch, there is a tombstone (View with PAP Visible). Interact with it to activate a "Zombie Blood" state (Your screen will turn red, but enemies can still see and attack you). This state is timed, and after a few minutes, the Zombie Blood will go away. In Solo, you can interact with the tombstone up to three times per round. In Co-op, all players can only interact with the stone once per round.
  3. While in the "Zombie Blood" state, locate a ghost lady somewhere around the map and escort her to the 'Mausoleum.' Zombie Blood doesn't last long enough to do the whole escort in one use. If you run out of "Zombie Blood" while escorting her, she will stop moving and wait until someone with Zombie Blood returns to escort her. Where she spawns is random, but below are some of the most common locations:
    • Entrance Hall
    • Master Bedroom
    • Music Room
    • Billiards Room
    • Greenhouse Laboratory, at the back
    • Dining Room
    • Stairs from 'Dining Room' to 'Master Bedroom'
  4. Once the ghost makes it to the 'Mausoleum,' four zombies (one at a time) will start to glow teal. These zombies need to be killed in a specific way in a specific order. When killed correctly, the ghost will appear above where the zombie died and a piece of jewellery will drop, which needs to be picked up before getting the next kill. The kill order requirements:
    1. Regular Kill (w/ any weapon)
    2. Fire Kill (Wraith Fire or Fire Trap)
    3. Special Weapon Kill
    4. Shield Bash or Gun Kill
  5. Once all four pieces have been picked up, place them on the white circles in the 'Mausoleum' to turn them blue. Once all four have been placed, a white pattern will appear on the ground.
  6. In the 'Mausoleum,' kill ~15 vampires. After every successful kill, you should hear the ghost lady scream. If you do not hear a scream, try getting the kills while standing on each of the white circles until one works.
  7. Once enough vampires have been killed, the white pattern on the ground should turn blue; once it is blue it only stays blue for a few seconds. You must then quickly interact with this tomb to open the 'Crypt' before the pattern returns to white.
  8. In the 'Crypt,' the Savage Impaler can be picked up on the far side of the 'Crypt,' which will activate a lockdown. Even though only one player can get the Savage Impaler from the quest, all players must be present to activate the lockdown.
  9. During the lockdown, Zombies, Vampires and a Werewolf will spawn that need to be killed. Once all the enemies are dead, the lockdown will end.

Stake Knife

The Stake Knife is a melee weapon which replaces the standard knife. It can one-hit kill vampires (crimson or not) on any round and one-hit kill zombies up to round 26.

The Stake Knife can be obtained from the following quest:

  1. Open Pack-a-Punch
  2. In the 'Forest,' there's a tree with a lantern on it near the Koshka wallbuy.
  3. Above the lantern, there is a symbol carved into the tree. There are also symbols on three trees nearby. Stand near the tree, so you are facing the lantern.
  4. When standing in the correct spot, the four symbols will line up allowing you to see them all at once (Brightened Example). Remember the symbols in order from left to right. Note: the symbols will be in a random order every game.
  5. In the 'Gardens,' there are four stones, each with one of the symbols from the trees on it. You must shoot the stones in the order the symbols were on the trees (left to right).
    • Each stone's symbol randomizes every new game.
    • Warning: If you shoot the stones in the wrong order, you must restart your game to retry. There is no way to respawn the stones.
    • Stone locations:
      • On the wall of the Gazebo
      • Behind the Vapr-XKG wallbuy
      • Right of the Perk Statue, behind a fountain
      • On the railing, right of the Zombie spawn
  6. If done correctly, your character will mention something about doing it in the right order.
  7. Buy the Bowie Knife from the Forest Terrace wallbuy.
  8. On a tree, left of the one with the lantern, there are the same four symbols etched around the circumference of the tree. Using the Bowie Knife, melee the symbols in the same order that they appeared on the trees. When a symbol is successfully meleed, a scratch will appear through it.
  9. Once all four symbols are meleed, melee each of the symbols on the tree (in any order this time) with the Ballistic Shield until a branch falls from it.
  10. Place the branch on the stone in the 'Mausoleum.'
  11. Kill ~15 Vampires near the branch to charge it.
  12. Once the branch is charged enough, it will no longer take vampire souls, the end will be smoking orange, and it can be picked up.
  13. Craft the Stake Knife at the workbench on the 'Cemetery Path.'

'Trial by Ordeal' Main Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 6/10, Est Completion Time - 1:10 Hr to 1:40 Hr

    Prerequisites

  1. Activate the Sentinel Artifact
  2. Activate Pack-a-Punch
  3. Interact with each of the three crystals from the Pack-a-Punch quest again.
    • Each crystal should show you one of three items: Telescope, Knight's Armour or Wooden Effigy.
    • Gazing into a crystal will activate the corresponding Quest Line in the Main Quest.
    • These Quest Lines can be completed in any order, and even at the same time (this is not recommended, specifically with the Effigy Quest Line, as it may cause some bugs).
  4. Note: Unlike other Easter Eggs in Black Ops 4, there is no text in the HUD to indicate the current step. Instead, on the Sentinel Artifact in the HUD, 12 Zodiac symbols light up when some steps are completed. These will be listed throughout the steps; the steps after the name will indicate how to make it light up in the hud.

    Telescope Quest line - Leo

  1. Interact with each stone until you see the Telescope to activate the Telescope Quest Line.

    2. Virgo

  2. Obtain Silver Bullets
  3. All players must shoot the Lightning Rod above the Mausoleum at the same time with Silver Bullets.
  4. If done correctly, it will sink into the Mausoleum and a telescope will take its place. The telescope should shoot a beam of light toward the Atlas Statue in the Mansion.

    5. Libra

  5. On the 'North Atrium Bridge,' there will be three boxes on the railing facing the Atlas Statue that will open when a player gets close to them. Open all the boxes to reveal the wheels inside.
  6. The goal is to block the three coloured beams coming from the crystal in the middle using the circular part of the rings. The wheels can be spun left by interacting with the left side or to the right by interacting with the right. When the wheels are spun, the rings Atlas is holding will spin. The easiest way to block all beams is:
    1. Spin the Middle Wheel to the Left until the Green light is covered
    2. Spin the Left Wheel to the Left until the Blue light is covered (this will uncover the green light)
    3. Spin the Right wheel to the Left until the Red light is covered
    4. Repeat Steps 1–3 until all lights are covered
  7. Once all lights are covered, the beams will melt the statue's head off.

    8. Scorpio

  8. Around the map, Zodiac Symbols will spawn in three of the following seven locations (Note: Capricorn on this image looks nothing like the one in-game). Each symbol has three sets of scratch marks associated with it. The scratch marks need to be added up to get the number for the Zodiac symbol (e.g. Aries - 3 Scratches, 5 Scratches and 2 Scratches would be Aries - 10).
    • What a symbol means is irrelevant, you only have to remember what the symbol looks like, and its associated number of scratches.
    • Sometimes the Zodiac symbols take a few seconds to load in, so stare at every location for a few seconds to make sure it loads.
    • If only two sets of scratches are visible for one symbol, consider the third (invisible) set of scratches to be zero.
    • Symbols and Scratches Locations:
      • Entrance Hall
        • Zodiac - Under a table, left of the Danu Statue --- Room View
        • Scratches - On the shield, right of the Danu Statue
        • Scratches - At the base of a column, right of the fast travel
        • Scratches - Right of the ship painting
      • Billiards Room
        • Zodiac - Under the table with the Chess Board --- Room View
        • Scratches - Left of the Bookcase closest to the 'Study'
        • Scratches - Above the cabinet, left of the clock
        • Scratches - Under the table, opposite the clock
      • Library
        • Zodiac - Beside a vase, left of the Lock Box
        • Scratches - Top right corner of Zombie spawn, left of the Zodiac symbol
        • Scratches - On a wooden box, right of the Ra statue
        • Scratches - At the base of the Skeleton, behind the Silver Bullets workbench
      • Main Hall
        • Zodiac - Behind a candle at the base of the stairs coming from the 'West Gallery'
        • Scratches - On the wall in the right fountain
        • Scratches - Above the fireplace closest to the Mozu
        • Scratches: North Atrium - When coming from the 'Forest,' in the top left corner
      • Wine Cellar
        • Zodiac - On the bottom shelf of the wine shelf, opposite the MX9 wallbuy
        • Scratches - On the big barrel at the bottom of the right stairs
        • Scratches - On the cabinet in the middle of the room, right from the left stairs
        • Scratches - On the top of a barrel, right of the workbench
      • Dining Room
        • Zodiac - On the door frame leading to the 'Greenhouse Laboratory'
        • Scratches - On the wall at the far end of the room
        • Scratches - On the end of the table, closest to the Clock
        • Scratches - On the ceiling on the other side of the wall from the Clock
      • Trophy Room
        • Zodiac - On the wall, right of the Case with Skulls inside
        • Scratches - Left side of the fireplace
        • Scratches: Master Bedroom - On the nightstand, left of the Bed
        • Scratches: Master Bedroom - Above the bathtub, outside the spawn window
  9. Once you have all three Zodiac symbols and their associated number of scratches, make your way to the Telescope, upstairs in the 'Greenhouse laboratory.'
  10. Below the telescope, there is a Dial with Zodiac symbols. You must input the symbols from the least number of scratches to most.
    • The dial can be spun left by interacting with the left side or to the right by interacting with the right.
    • When a symbol is at the top of the dial, melee it to input the symbol.
    • If you don't input the correct symbols or input them in the wrong order, the symbols and scratches will reset, and you will have to find them again.
  11. If done correctly, the telescope will begin to move.
  12. Obtain the Ballistic Shield
  13. While holding the shield, hold the interact button on this Wheel near the telescope until Part of the Roof opens. Once it is fully open, melee the wheel with the shield. If done correctly, the lights above the wheel will turn green and the roof will stay open.
  14. Activate the Electric Trap below the telescope. All players must run through it while holding the shield to electrify it.
  15. All players must then melee the Eyepiece of the Telescope with the electrified shield at the same time. If done correctly, the screen with flash white and a beam will shoot out of the end of the Telescope into the sky.
  16. To the left of the Electric Trap, a part of the wall will open to reveal a Stone. All players must hold interact on the stone until it flies out to activate a lockdown.
  17. During the lockdown, Zombies and Vampires will endlessly spawn. After a couple of minutes, a few Werewolves will spawn. Once all the Werewolves are killed, the screen will flash white, and you will be able to pick up the Stone. When you pick up the Stone, a Max Ammo will spawn.

    Effigy Quest line - Sagittarius

  1. Interact with each stone until you see the Effigy to activate the Effigy Quest Line.

    2. Capricorn

  2. Obtain the Alistair's Annihilator

    3. If you are having trouble with the branches or finding/activating the tombstone, make sure you are not in the middle of another quest line as this can halt the progress of this quest line. If you are, complete the other quest line then come back to this one.

  3. In the 'Cemetery' area, there are five birch trees that will drop leaves when a player gets close. Shoot the trunks of these trees with Alistair's Annihilator to make them drop a Branch. Interact with this branch to make it fly toward the center of the 'Cemetery.'
    • The branches are a bit finicky and sometimes don't fall when shot or fly to the middle when picked up. You can check how many were collected by looking in front of the Mystery Box location in the 'Cemetery.'
    • Also, sometimes, despite flying away when picked up, they aren't counted as being picked up. If this happens, go around to each location and hold interact on where the branch would be on the ground.
    • Tree Locations:
      • Cemetery Path - On the left when coming from the Mansion
      • Cemetery - Right of the Crimson Nosferatu's Coffin
      • Cemetery - Near the top of the right staircase, coming from the 'Cemetery Path'
      • Mausoleum - On the front right side of the Mausoleum area, near the stairs
      • Mausoleum - Back left of the Mausoleum area, left of the fast travel

    Aquarius

  4. Around the 'Cemetery,' there will be a tombstone with one of the characters and the year "1912" on it. If solo, interact with the tombstone to activate "Green Vision". If co-op, only the player playing as the character shown on the tombstone can activate "Green Vision", if that character is not in the game, anyone can activate it.
  5. In the Green Vision, the five branches will form an effigy. You must shoot a Charged Fireball Shot from Alistair's Annihilator at the base of the effigy and hold interact on the effigy.
  6. A short cutscene will start where your character will attach themselves to the effigy and be sucked down into "Hell". After the cutscene, you will be returned to the map as a "ghost", as indicated by the blue effect on the screen.
    • If you go down while in Ghost Mode, you will need to reactivate it by re-entering Green Vision, shooting the Charged Fireball Shot and interacting with the Effigy.
  7. While in Ghost Mode, you must find and escort a ghost lady to the 'Cemetery.' She can spawn in random locations around the map, but some of the more common locations are below:
    • Entrance Hall - Near/Inside the Danu Perk Statue
    • Wine Cellar - Inside the right-most Zombie spawn
    • Master Bedroom - Inside the Zombie spawn
    • Dining Room - Behind the table at the end of the room
  8. Once you find the ghost, escort her back to the Mystery Box location in the 'Cemetery.' Once she gets there, she will erupt into flames and drop a Stone.

    9. Pisces

  9. Interact with the Stone to exit Ghost Mode. All players must then hold interact on the stone until it floats up to activate a lockdown.
  10. During the lockdown, Zombies and Vampires will endlessly spawn. After a couple of minutes, a few Werewolves will spawn. Once all the Werewolves are killed, the screen will flash white, and you will be able to pick up the Stone. When you pick up the Stone, a Max Ammo will spawn.

    Knight Quest line - Aries

  1. Interact with each stone until you see the Knight to activate the Knight Quest Line.

    2. Taurus

  2. Obtain the Alistair's Annihilator and Ballistic Shield
  3. Activate the Fire Trap in the 'Library' (and the one in the 'Master Bedroom' to do the third set if you'd like).
  4. Turn on a trap and shoot the flames from the trap with a Charged shot from Alistair's Annihilator to turn them blue.
  5. While the flames are blue, run through them while holding the shield to light it on fire.
  6. With the flaming shield, you must melee a set of fireplaces around the map in a certain order.
    • The sets can be done in any order, but the order of the fireplaces in a set must always be done in the order shown below.

Hidden Rooms / Various Free Items

Three secret rooms can be opened, revealing either a powerup (which doesn't despawn), a weapon, an elixir or free Silver Bullets.

If you fail to open a room, you must restart your game to be able to try again.

Billiards Room

  1. In the 'Billiards Room,' shoot the billiard balls into any pocket in ascending order (1 to 9). If the balls were pocketed in the wrong order, a grenade would spawn.
  2. Once all the balls are in pockets, either a bonus points powerup will spawn from the table or the bookshelf will slide open revealing your reward.

Wine Cellar

  1. In the 'Wine Cellar,' there are six barrels dripping wine. Locations:
    • In the corner, right of the workbench
    • Right of the previous barrel
    • In the middle of the room, near the workbench
    • Left of MX9 wallbuy
    • Bottom of the stairs, farthest from the workbench
    • Right of MX9 wallbuy
  2. The barrels must be meleed in order of how fast they are dripping, the fastest first. If the correct barrel is meleed, it will produce a wine splash effect. Otherwise, if the wrong one was meleed, no effect will play.
  3. If the barrels are meleed in the correct order, the shelf will slide open revealing your reward.

East/West Balcony

  1. Locate the bookshelves on the 'East Balcony' and 'West Balcony'
  2. Four of the books (two on each bookshelf) will have one of the following symbols on them. You must interact with them in the following order:
    1. Symbol 1
    2. Symbol 2
    3. Symbol 3
    4. Symbol 4
  3. Once the books have been interacted with, the bookshelf with the last book will slide open revealing your reward.

Jump Scare

  • Repeatedly interact with one of the crystals (Purple shown as an example, any colour works) used to open Pack-a-Punch until the jumpscare activates.

Infinite Ammo (Images Incomplete)

The rewards for this easter egg are infinite ammo on one weapon for the rest of the game, all loadout perks as well as two random perks for free and free Homunculus equipment.

This easter egg is very complicated and requires your full attention from the beginning of the game. It is not recommended to try and start it "mid-game".

Also, Wraith Fire Equipment is required to complete this easter egg

  1. Activate the Sentinel Artifact
  2. Open the following doors in this order (it doesn't have to be this order as long as you remember which of these doors you opened in what order):
    1. 'Main Hall' to 'Wine Cellar'
    2. 'Main Hall' to 'Dining Room'
    3. 'Dining Room' to 'Greenhouse'
    4. 'Main Hall' to 'Library'
    5. 'Library' to 'Cemetery'
    6. 'Main Hall' to 'Forest'
  3. Look (aim and stare) at the Gramophones, for about five seconds, in the following order (there is no sound or cue that you have successfully "looked" at a gramophone):
    1. Music Room
    2. West Gallery
    3. Master Bedroom
    4. Dining Room
    5. Library
    6. Behind the bookshelf from the East/West Balcony Hidden Room
  4. Obtain a Pack-a-Punched M1897 Trebuchet. It can be purchased from the wallbuy in the 'Library'.
  5. There are six vases around the mansion (each linked to one of the doors from Step 1) that need to be broken.
    • To break a vase, you need to perfectly land a shot from the upgraded M1897 Trebuchet into the hole on top of the vase.
    • If you break the vases in the wrong order, you have to start a new game to retry.
    • Vase Locations:
      1. 'Main Hall' to 'Wine Cellar' Door - Off the left side of the 'Grand Staircase,' in the water --- Shoot From: Grand Staircase, through the railing
      2. 'Main Hall' to 'Dining Room' Door - Above the 'East Balcony' (The lower of the two) --- Line up location: Stand Here --- Aim Here
      3. 'Dining Room' to 'Greenhouse' Door - Above the 'East Balcony' (The higher of the two) --- Shoot From: West Balcony
      4. 'Main Hall' to 'Library' Door - Above the 'West Balcony' --- Shoot From: Bottom of the 'Grand Staircase,' in the 'Main Hall'
      5. 'Library' to 'Cemetery' Door - Above the 'West Gallery' --- Shoot From: East Balcony
      6. 'Main Hall' to 'Forest' Door - Above 'North Atrium Bridge' (Spawn) --- Line up location: Stand Here --- Aim Here
  6. If all vases were destroyed correctly, this candle in the 'Forest Terrace' should no longer be lit.
  7. There are six candles around the map (each linked to one of the doors from Step 1) that need to be ignited with the Wraith Fire equipment.
    • These candles need to be ignited in the reverse order of how the doors were opened.
    • If you light the candles in the wrong order, you have to start a new game to retry.
    • Candle Locations:
      1. Forest Terrace - 'Main Hall' to 'Forest' Door
      2. Study - 'Library' to 'Cemetery' Door
      3. West Hallway - 'Main Hall' to 'Library' Door
      4. Greenhouse Terrace - 'Dining Room' to 'Greenhouse' Door
      5. East Hallway - 'Main Hall' to 'Dining Room' Door
      6. Wine Cellar - 'Main Hall' to 'Wine Cellar' Door
  8. In the 'East Stairway,' pick up the Vinyl Record leaning against a cabinet.
  9. You must play the record at the gramophones from step 2 in the reverse order of how you looked at them.
    • When placed, the record will play part of the map's secret song Mystery.
    • If you play the record at the gramophones in the wrong order, you have to start a new game to retry.
    • Gramophone Locations:
      1. Behind the bookshelf from the East/West Balcony Hidden Room
      2. Library
      3. Dining Room
      4. Master Bedroom
      5. West Gallery
      6. Music Room
  10. Once all the gramophones have been played, look at this window in the 'North Atrium' to spawn an invisible ghost which can't be seen but can be heard.
  11. The ghost needs to be escorted around the map, the player has to listen for the sounds the ghost makes to know where it is.
  12. Eventually, you will make your way to the 'Wine Cellar' (make sure to hold out the weapon you'd like infinite ammo for). Once you reach the bottom of the stairs, you must go prone and crawl over to this broken barrel following this path.
  13. If everything is done correctly, you will be given your rewards. (Was it worth it?)
