Sniper Cabinet / Scoped Sniper Wallbuy (WAW, BO1, BO3)

  • The Scoped Kar98k can be purchased from the cabinet on the second floor, above the Mystery Box room for 1500 points. Once the cabinet is open, ammo can be purchased for 380 points.
  • On the Zombies Chronicles version (BO3), this wallbuy is replaced by a Locus wallbuy.

Song Radio / Various Songs (WAW, BO1, BO3)

  • Damage (shoot or melee) the radio on the table right of the Mystery Box. You can shoot it again to skip the current song.

    'Samantha's Lullaby' Song + Free Max Ammo (BO3)

    Link to song on streaming here.

    1. Interact with the four buttons around the map in any order. Locations:
      1. Ground Floor - On the side of a pillar, left of Mule Kick
      2. Ground Floor - Under the shelf at the bottom of the stairs in the Mystery Box Room
      3. Second Floor - In the Wunderfizz room, on the other side of the room, on the wall across from the Zombie spawn window
      4. Second Floor - On the ceiling above the stairs in spawn
    2. Once all four are interacted with, a sound will play, and a Samantha doll will appear on the floor in front of the RK5 wallbuy. Interact with the Doll to make it disappear, and spawn five (one at a time) others around the map. The doll you're looking for will make a sound when you're close to it.
    3. Shoot five Sam dolls around the map. The Dolls spawn one at a time and play a little song to help you locate them.
      • Taking too long to shoot a Doll will make it despawn, a Samantha giggle sound will play, and you will fail the step. You can retry by interacting with the Sam doll by the RK5 as soon as it respawns.
      • Locations:
        1. Ground Floor - On a fence post out the window diagonal from Mule Kick
        2. Ground Floor - In the pond, out the window across from the RK5 wallbuy
        3. Second Floor - Next to a power generator, out the window at the top of the spawn room stairs
        4. Ground Floor - On top of a truck, outside the window right of the Sheiva wallbuy
        5. Ground Floor - On the end of the tank's barrel, out the window next to Mule Kick
        6. Second Floor - On top of a telephone pole, out the broken wall near the Cabinet
        7. Second Floor - In the hole in the ceiling, the left of the Wunderfizz
        8. Ground Floor - In the hole in the ceiling in front of Mule Kick (Stand in front of Mule Kick and jump)
    4. If the five dolls were shot in time, the doll in front of the RK5 will now be standing. Interact with it to spawn a Max Ammo, and play the song.
