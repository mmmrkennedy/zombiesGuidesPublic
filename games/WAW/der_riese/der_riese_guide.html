Der Riese Guide

Pack-a-Punch

  1. Activate the Power by interacting with the Power Switch.
  2. Around the map, there are three teleporters that need to be linked to the mainframe in spawn. There will be one teleporter at the "end" of each area. Interact with a teleporter to start the link process; a clock will appear at the top left of your screen counting down to 30 seconds. You must get to the mainframe before the clock reaches zero. Teleporter locations:
    • Teleporter A - Left of spawn
    • Teleporter B - Right of spawn
    • Teleporter C - Power Switch Area
  3. As you link teleporters, the door on the mainframe will lower slightly. After the last is linked, the door will open, and Pack-a-Punch will become accessible.

Paradoxical Prologue (Fly Trap) Main Quest / Annihilator Special Weapon (BO3)

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 1/10, Est Completion Time - 5 to 20 Mins

  1. Unlock Pack-a-Punch and get a bullet-based paped weapon.
  2. Shoot the control panel out the Zombie spawn on the right of the map (when facing right of PAP) with the paped weapon. Multiple items should start flying around it, then fly into the air.
  3. Three of these items will spawn around the map which you must damage (shoot, melee, grenade, etc). Locations:
    • Teleporter A - In a furnace left of the right-most stairs
    • Power Switch Area - On a fire escape left of the Power Switch (toward OG Jug spawn), near the top of the building
    • Teleporter B - In the left water tank (when entering)
  4. Once all three items are shot, Maxis will speak, and after some dialogue, the Annihilator Special Weapon will appear in the furnace in the room "below" teleporter B.

'Beauty of Annihilation' Song (Remix Version in BO3)

  1. There are three brain jars that need to be interacted with to start the song. Jar Locations:
    • Animal Testing (left side) - On a shelf near the bottom of the stairs to Jug
    • Animal Testing (left side) - Across from the previous locations, directly to the right of the stairs to Jug
    • Teleporter B - In the side room, right of the teleporter, on a table beside the chalkboard
  2. Once all three jars are interacted with, the song will start.

New Perk Machine (Deadshot or Stamin-Up) - BO3

  1. Unlock Pack-a-Punch and get Monkey Bombs from the Mystery Box.
  2. Throw a Monkey Bomb into a teleporter and teleport before the Monkey goes off. If done correctly, one of the red lights on the panel on a railing beside the mainframe will turn green.
  3. Once you teleport at all three teleporters, you can interact with the panel to activate the Giant's eye beam. The beam will melt a block of snow, revealing either the Deadshot, or Stamin-Up perk machine on the other side of the Spawn room wall.
