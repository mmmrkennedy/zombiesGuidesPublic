'Lullaby of a Deadman' Song (WAW, BO1, BO3)

Link to song on streaming here.

  • In the room with the toilets near the power room on the Double Tap side, interact with the left-most toilet three times in a row. After the third flush, the song should start playing.

Song Radio / Campaign Songs (BO1)

  • Shoot the radio on the desk in the Zombie spawn window on the balcony above the Mule Kick room.

'Samantha's Lullaby' Song + Free Max Ammo (BO3)

Link to song on streaming here.

  1. Interact with the bowl of the right-most toilet nine times, the centre toilet three times, then the left-most toilet five times. If done correctly, you should hear a sound kind of like a scream.
  2. A Samantha doll will spawn on the ground to the left of the Wunderfizz machine. Interact with the Doll to make it disappear, and spawn five (one at a time) others around the map. The doll you're looking for will make a sound when you're close to it.
    • Taking too long to shoot a Doll will make it despawn, a Samantha giggle sound will play, and you will fail the step. You can retry by interacting with the Sam doll by the RK5 as soon as it respawns.
    • Locations:
      1. Courtyard - On top of the bloody fountain in the middle of the map
      2. Kitchen - In a pot
      3. Speed Cola Room - Out the Zombie spawn barrier, on the railing
      4. Mule Kick Room - Up the stairs from Mule Kick, on the flaming hole in the ceiling, hanging from the horizontal support pillar
      5. Mule Kick Room - Hanging from the ceiling inside a barrier
      6. Quick Revive Spawn Room - In the open mortuary cabinet
      7. Juggernog Spawn Room - On the dentist chair
      8. Double Tap Hallway - On top of a mannequin at the top of the stairs from spawn
      9. Wunderfizz Room - In the right-most toilet
      10. Power Room - On the floor in the barrier down the stairs
  3. If the five dolls were shot in time, the doll in near the Wunderfizz will now be standing. Interact with it to spawn a Max Ammo, and play the song.
