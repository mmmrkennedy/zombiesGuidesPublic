Power

  • There is only one Power Switch, and it's in CHANGE ME.

Pack-a-Punch

  1. Turn on the Power.
  2. There are three pieces of the Pack-a-Punch machine scattered around the map. Interacting with one of these pieces will teleport it away. Piece locations:
    • Bastion - In the area with the Death Ray trap, outside the power room
    • Undercroft - At the back of the room, between the Jug and Teleporter rooms
    • Rocket Pad - On the elevated platform surrounding the rocket
  3. Once you interact with the third piece, the Pack-a-Punch machine will form at that location. Randomly, every 2-3 rounds, the machine will move to one of the other locations (where the pieces were). The machine's current location is indicated by the light on the sign near each location.

Undercroft Low-Gravity

  1. To activate Anti-Gravity, you'll need to stand on each of the four platforms in the Undercroft (Pyramid Room) for 3 seconds. After 3 seconds, the platform will until they start glowing, indicating it's activated.
  2. Once all 4 platforms are activated, the room will light up, and you'll be able to jump higher and wallrun. Zombies can also jump up to you, and they can hit you midair. Low gravity lasts for 50 seconds (the lights in the room will begin flashing when 10 seconds is remaining). Low gravity will reactivate after 60 seconds.

Wunderspheres

  • The Wunderspheres are a fast travel mechanic that, when activated, will fling any players standing inside the sphere to the corresponding landing pad. You'll need to activate the landing pad before you can activate that pad's Wundersphere. Each Wundersphere always has the same landing pad location.

Rocket Test Pad

  • Down the mountain from the Castle, there is an active Rocket Test Pad. This area can be reached by either using the Teleporter in the Undercroft or the Wundersphere in the Bastion/Death Ray area (landing pad on the test pad must be activated first). Since the Wundersphere needs to be activated, you'll always need to use the Teleporter the first time you go.
  • Anywhere from 30 seconds to 4 minutes after the first teleport, the Rocket will begin testing, indicated by an announcement from Maxis (that only players at the pad can hear).
  • Once you hear the announcement, you have 10 seconds to either leave the Rocket Pad via the Wundersphere to spawn or "hide" in the bunker with the Teleporter. During the countdown, the doors to the bunker will slowly start to close.
  • Any players still on the pad when the countdown ends will be instantly killed (not downed). On Solo, if you have Quick Revive, you'll be downed and teleported into the bunker instead of killed.
  • After the first test, the Rocket will go on cooldown for anywhere from 3.5 to 7 minutes before another test.

Rocket Shield

  1. The Rocket Shield requires three parts to be built: the Rockets, Base, and Window.
    • Rockets:
      • Lower Courtyard - On the wooden boxes on the right, past the CHANGE ME trap
      • Lower Courtyard - Just up the stairs from the last location, at the base of the small stairs
      • Lower Courtyard - At the top of the stairs leading to the Death Ray trap platform
    • Base:
      • Clocktower - Leaning against a desk
      • Upper Courtyard - At the bottom of the stairs, coming from the Wunderfizz location
      • Upper Courtyard - Leaning against some boxes, right of the electric trap
    • Window - Requires Low Gravity to be active:
      • Undercroft - Above the Storm Box box
      • Undercroft - Above the Pack-a-Punch piece location
      • Undercroft - Above the doorway to the Teleporter room
  2. Once you have all three parts, the Rocket Shield can be crafted at the Crafting Table in the Gatehouse, Church, or Undercroft.

Ragnarok DG-4 Specialist Weapon

  1. The Ragnarok DG-4s requires three parts to be built: the Handles, Spikes, and Base.
    • Base:
      • Dropped by the first Panzersoldat killed; first spawns around round 12.
    • Handles
      1. Turn on the Power and activate the Wundersphere landing pads in the Upper and Lower Courtyards.
      2. Activate the Death Ray trap located just outside the Power room, and wait for it to be done.
      3. Once the trap is finished, the handles will either spawn in the air over one of the Death Ray "orbs," or behind the Wundersphere to the Rocket Pad. Based on this location, you'll need to fling yourself at it using one of the Wunderspheres below:
        • Orb - Upper Courtyard
        • Rocket Pad - Lower Courtyard/Gatehouse
      4. If done correctly, you pick up the part as you fly into it.
      5. If you take too long to grab the part, CHANGE ME.
    • Spikes:
      1. Wait at the Rocket Test Pad, near the inactive switch by the stairs up to the Pack-a-Punch platform.
      2. Once a Rocket test starts, interact with the newly activated switch (may take a few seconds to activate), then quickly run into the bunker.
      3. In the bunker, left of the Teleporter, there's a panel with three lights and a switch. Wait until all three lights are green, then interact with the switch.
      4. If done correctly, the spikes will spawn in the Teleporter for you to pick up.
      5. If you take too long to grab the part, it'll despawn, and you'll need to try again.
  2. Once you have all three parts, the Ragnarok DG-4s can be crafted at the Crafting Table in the Gatehouse, Church, or Undercroft.

Wrath of The Ancients Bow

  1. Around the map, there are three Dragon statues you need to feed. They can be fed in any order and simultaneously.
    • The first Dragon statue is in the Lower Courtyard, near the first gate trap.
    • The second Dragon statue is in the Mission Control room (Church), above the fireplace.
    • The third Dragon statue is in the Undercroft, near the Pyramid.
  2. Kill a zombie near a statue to bring it to life. Feed the dragon by killing zombies near it; it requires eight zombies to be full.
    • Make sure to wait for the dragon to finish chewing and swallowing a zombie before killing the next one; otherwise, the second zombie will not be eaten and will be wasted.
  3. Once full, the dragon will crumble into rubble, indicating it is complete. Repeat for all three statues.
  4. In the room connecting the Undercroft and the Church, the Bow will have spawned in the chest behind the Knight's Tomb.
  5. Interact with the chest to pick up the Wrath of the Ancients bow. All players can pick up the Bow.

Storm Bow (Kreema'ahm la Ahmahm)

The uncharged shot spawns a small electric orb that lasts a few seconds, killing or damaging any enemies that walk into it. A charged shot spawns a lightning storm that kills any Zombies who enter.

  1. Obtain the Wrath of the Ancients Bow.
  2. On the Death Ray platform, shoot a charged shot at the weather vane on the small tower. The vane should spin, and the Lightning Arrow will drop to the floor below it.
  3. Pick up the Arrow to start the Storm Bow quest.
    • The quest is "bound" to you while you hold the arrow; only the player with the arrow can complete the steps. Picking up another bow's arrow will swap quests, returning your original arrow to its spawn location, allowing another player to take over the quest where you left off.
  4. Shoot a charged shot from the Wrath of the Ancients Bow at the three unlit bonfires located outside the map to light them:
    • Castle - Behind the Clocktower
    • Castle - On a far-off mountain, "behind" the Wunderfizz machine
    • Rocket Test Pad - On the hill, up toward the Castle
  5. Once all three bonfires are lit, go to the Undercroft and wait for Low Gravity to start. Once Low Gravity is active, you must wallrun on all five "wind" panels without touching the ground. Running over a panel will light it up. Panel locations:
    • CHANGE ME
  6. Once all five are lit, CHANGE ME.
  7. Now, you need to shoot each Bonfire again, this time with an electrified arrow. To electrify the arrow, charge a nearby Urn with 5 kills; a charged Urn will stop collecting souls and start sparking with electricity. Hold a charged shot with the Bow near the Urn to electrify the arrow. There are three Urns, one for each Bonfire.
    • You have infinite attempts to hit the bonfire; the Urn will not "go out."
    • Once you hit a Bonfire, the Urn will go out, and you can move to the next one.
    • Bonfire/Urn Locations:
      • Behind the Clocktower - Urn: Clocktower, under the stairs
      • Far-off Mountain - Urn: Gate Building, left of the LCAR-9 wallbuy (above Double Tap)
      • Rocket Test Pad - Urn: Bunker, near the rubble in front of the Teleporter
  8. Once all three Bonfires are electrified, interact with the wall below the Weather Vane (where you picked up the arrow). After a short animation, the arrow will come back fully formed; pick it up.
  9. Place it in the Storm Bow box by the Pyramid in the Undercroft. A Max Ammo will spawn.
  10. Kill zombies while they are standing on the stairs or the lower area around the Pyramid to charge the Arrow.
  11. Once the arrow is fully charged, interact with the box to give it your unupgraded bow. After a short animation, the Storm Bow will appear for you to pick up.

Wolf Bow (Kreeholo lu Kreemasaleet)

  1. Obtain the Wrath of the Ancients Bow.
  2. Interact with four paintings around the map in a specific order to tell the story of the Wolf King. The locations of the paintings are randomized each game, but the order you must interact with them is always the same based on the image depicted.
    • Order of Images:
      1. The King sitting on his Throne
      2. The King riding a horse into battle
      3. Creature in the sky
      4. The King dead with arrows in him
    • Painting Locations:
      • Clocktower - Ground floor, inside the tower
      • Clocktower - Top floor, near the stairs
      • Church - Upper level, near the Speed Cola/M87 wallbuy
      • Samantha's Room - In the room above Mule Kick/Knight's tomb
  3. If you interact with the correct painting, you will hear a guitar strum. If incorrect, you will hear a buzzer sound and must restart the sequence.
  4. Once all four paintings are activated in order, you will hear a wolf howl.
  5. Go to the Undercroft. The wall at the bottom of the stairs from the room with the Wrath of the Ancients will break open, and the broken Wolf arrow will fall out. Pick up the Broken Arrow.
  6. Head to the Rocket Test Pad area. Look up at the mountain to the right of the rocket, above/behind the VMP wallbuy. There is a red flag high up on the cliff. Shoot the rock directly below the flag with your bow. If hit correctly, a Wolf Skull will roll down the cliff and land near the gate. Pick it up.
  7. Return to the Undercroft and interact with the headless skeleton beside the Wolf Bow box near the Pyramid to place the skull.
  8. A Ghost Wolf will spawn. Follow the wolf as it travels around the map. The wolf will stop at three locations to dig for bones. When it stops and digs, kill zombies near the wolf to feed it souls. It takes CHANGE ME kills to fill the spot.
    • Lower Courtyard - Near the gate to the spawn
    • Double Tap Area - On the path leading up to the mystery box/Wundersphere
    • Undercroft - Directly in front of the Pack-a-Punch machine location
  9. Once the wolf finishes digging at a location, it will unearth a bone. You must pick up the bone to continue.
  10. After collecting all three bones, the wolf will return to the Undercroft and sit in front of a wall near the Jug room.
  11. Wait for Anti-Gravity to activate (cycles every minute or so).
  12. Once gravity is low, wallrun across the wall the wolf is looking at. While wallrunning, a Wolf Symbol (two dog heads) will glow on the wall. Shoot this symbol with your bow while still on the wall (do not touch the ground).
  13. A platform will spawn from the wall. Land on the platform. Interact with the skeleton in the hole in the wall to pick up the Reforged Arrow.
    • If you miss the jump or the platform times out, simply wallrun and shoot the symbol again to respawn it.
  14. Place the arrow in the Wolf Bow box by the Pyramid in the Undercroft. A Max Ammo will spawn.
  15. Kill zombies near the box to charge the Arrow with souls.
  16. Once the arrow is fully charged, interact with the box to give it your unupgraded bow. After a short animation, the Wolf Bow will appear for you to pick up.

Wolf Bow: A charged shot fires a spectral wolf that runs forward, killing zombies in a wide path and slowing/pushing back enemies.

Void Bow (Kreegakaleet lu Gosata'ahm)

  1. Obtain the Wrath of the Ancients Bow.
  2. Head to the Double Tap area (Gate House) and look for a purple symbol on the wall/ceiling above the gate. Shoot it with a charged shot from your bow to break the wall. A broken purple arrow will drop to the floor; pick it up.
  3. Go to the KRM wallbuy room directly below the Clock Tower. Melee kill a zombie with the bow's melee attack while it's standing directly over the glowing purple square tile on the floor in the center of the room.
    • The melee has to come from the bow's arrow melee attack; you can't use your normal knife or the Bowie Knife.
  4. If done correctly, the tile will break and an Urn will float up into the air.
  5. You must now interact with six Keeper Skulls scattered around the map:
    • Upper Courtyard - On a window sill on the stairs leading up to the Church
    • Mule Kick - On a small broken wall/ledge in the Mule Kick room
    • Double Tap - On a broken ledge/wall next to the Double Tap machine
    • Samantha's Room - Inside the open toy box in Samantha's Bedroom
    • Undercroft (Teleporter Room) - Inside the sink/basin on the right side of the room
    • Rocket Pad - In the back of a truck, to the left of the Rocket Launch Pad
  6. Return to the room with the Urn. You must create six Crawlers and bring them into the room. When a crawler enters the purple circle on the floor, the floating skulls will eat it.
  7. Once the skulls have eaten the crawlers, the Urn will speak three names to you (e.g., "Crown, Stag, Heart"). The possible words are: Crown, Stag, Heart, Door, Griffin, Horn.
    • If you miss the names, shoot the Urn with your bow and it will repeat them.
    • If you enable subtitles in the CHANGE ME settings, the words the Urn says will be shown in the subtitles.
  8. Go to the Knight's Hall (the room connecting Samantha's Room and Power). Look at the base of the statues to find a symbol that matches the words you heard. Interact with the correct statue to reveal its corresponding Apothicon Symbol. Memorize which Apothicon Symbol goes with which word.
  9. Kill zombies anywhere on the map until they drop purple symbols. You need to collect the specific symbols that match the names the Urn gave you. The symbols drop often, but disappear quickly, so pick them up immediately.
  10. Return to the Urn room. The symbols you collected will now appear on the floor tiles. Shoot the symbols on the floor with your bow in the exact order the Urn spoke the names.
  11. If incorrect, the voice will say "That is not my name" and you will be attacked by skulls if you stay in the room; you must try again (shoot the Urn to hear the order again). If done correctly, the voice will say "That is my name." Interact with the blue beam of light/Urn to spawn the Reforged Arrow; pick it up.
  12. Head to the Undercroft and place the arrow in the Void Bow box by the Pyramid. A Max Ammo will spawn.
  13. Kill zombies near the box to charge it with souls.
  14. Once charged, interact with the box to give it your unupgraded bow. After a short animation, the Void Bow will appear for you to pick up.

Void Bow: Charged Shot fires a portal that traps and kills zombies, and spawns skulls that attack enemies.

Fire Bow (Kreeaho'ahm nal Ahmhogaroc)

  1. Obtain the Wrath of the Ancients Bow.
  2. Head to the Clocktower. Go to the top of the stairs (inside the tower) and fire a charged shot at the symbol on the wall. The wall should break open. The broken Fire Arrow will appear; pick it up to begin the quest.
  3. Go to the Rocket Test Pad area and wait for a Rocket Test launch to begin. Stay inside the bunker/tunnel while the test occurs to survive.
  4. As soon as the test finishes and the blast doors open, run out and look to the right. There will be a glowing orange rock on the structure above/behind the VMP wallbuy. Shoot the glowing part of this rock with your bow. If done correctly, a ball of fire will launch into the sky and settle above the Bastion/Death Ray.
  5. You must now activate three ritual circles by shooting them with your bow while mid-air using a Wundersphere. You must hit the center of the circle while flying. If hit, the circle will stay lit up orange even when you aren't mid-air.
    • Double Tap - The circle is on the ground near the Double Tap machine/Landing Pad
    • Death Ray - The circle is on the ground near the Death Ray trap
    • Clocktower/Courtyard - The circle is on the ground in the Upper Courtyard, outside the Clocktower
  6. Once a circle is lit, stand inside it and kill CHANGE ME zombies to fill the circle with souls. Once complete, a beam of fire will shoot up into the sky. Repeat for all three circles.
  7. Return to the Clocktower and interact with the clock mechanism (where you got the arrow) at the top of the stairs. The clock gears will spin and stop, revealing a Keeper Symbol. This symbol will be present in one of three Fireplaces on the map.
    • Church - At the back of the Church (Speed Cola), left of the workbench
    • Richtofen's Room - Across from Samantha's Room, CHANGE ME of the Wunderfizz location
    • Upper Courtyard - In the room up the stairs from the (first) Dragon head with the Vesper wallbuy
  8. One of the three ritual circles will still be glowing (the other two will have gone out); go to that circle.
  9. You must now shoot a "magma rock" from the ritual circle to the correct fireplace. Shoot a charged shot while standing in the circle towards the general direction of the fireplace. A small volcano/magma rock will spawn where the arrow lands. Stand on the new magma rock and shoot again to move it closer. You have a maximum of 4 shots to get the rock into the fireplace.
    • Double Tap Circle → Goes to Samantha's Room Fireplace
    • Death Ray Circle → Goes to Church Fireplace
    • Clocktower Circle → Goes to Upper Courtyard/Trophy Room Fireplace
  10. Once the rock lands in the fireplace, interact with the fireplace to place your arrow inside. The arrow will be reforged; pick it up from the fireplace.
  11. Head to the Death Ray and look for the giant ball of fire floating in the sky. Interact with the ball of fire.
    • The location you have to stand to be able to interact with the fireball is a little finicky, so keep moving around until you see the prompt.
  12. The ball will explode, leaving the Reforged Arrow for you to pick up.
  13. Head to the Undercroft and place the arrow in the Fire Bow box by the Pyramid. A Max Ammo will spawn.
  14. Kill zombies near the box to charge it with souls.
  15. Once charged, interact with the box to give it your unupgraded bow. After a short animation, the Fire Bow will appear for you to pick up.

Fire Bow: Charged Shot traps zombies in volcanic rocks/magma before exploding.
