'Flotsam & Jetsam' Main Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - CHANGE ME, Est. Completion Time - CHANGE ME, RNG Rating - CHANGE ME

  1. Power the Weapon Disposal Machine

  2. Purchase the Teleport Grenades from the wallbuy in the Armoury.
  3. Activate the Chompy trap in the Cargo Bay. You need to teleport into Chompy's room using the teleport grenades. This can be done when the door opens either when Chompy is first activated, or when it ends.
  4. While in this room, interact with the Power Generator. You'll be teleported out shortly after.
  5. The Weapon Disposal Machine in the Armoury (right of the CHANGE ME wallbuy) should now have a Zombie arm sticking out of it.

  6. Build the Fishing Rod

  7. You'll now need to give the Weapon Disposal Machine weapons. Every second weapon you give the Machine will give you a Quest item. You'll get the following items in this order, starting with the second weapon: Glass Bottle (will fall to the floor; don't interact with it yet), Fishing Reel, Fishing Line, and Fishing Hook.
  8. With any of the Fishing Rod parts, go to the Gun Deck and interact with the pole on the edge of the ship to add the part.
  9. Once all parts are added, you'll be able to fish for items for CHANGE ME points.

  10. Retrieve 4 Data Tablets

  11. The remainder of this Main Quest consists of obtaining tablets. They must be collected in the following order:

  12. Tablet 1 - Grenade Disposal Game

    1. Shoot a teleport grenade into the Grenade Disposal Machine in the Armoury.
