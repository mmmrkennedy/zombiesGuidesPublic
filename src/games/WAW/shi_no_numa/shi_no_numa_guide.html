Easter Eggs

  • 'The One' Song (WAW, BO1, BO3)
  • 'Samantha's Sorrow' Song + Free Max Ammo (BO3)

'The One' Song (WAW, BO1, BO3)

  • Interact with the phone in the Comms Room three times in a row. After the third time, the song should start playing.

'Samantha's Sorrow' Song + Free Max Ammo (BO3)

Link to song on streaming here.

  1. Shoot four silver plates/pans hanging from the walls in the Fishing Hut with the 1911 starting pistol in any order.
    • If done correctly, you'll hear a Samantha scream. If you take too long, CHANGE ME.
    • Pan locations:
      • Plate 1
      • Plate 2
      • Plate 3
      • Plate 4
  2. Pick up Samantha Doll on the ground in the side room of the Fishing Hut.
  3. Shoot the five or six Samantha's dolls on the bridge outside the Fishing Hut, they'll spawn one at a time.
  4. Interact with the Samantha's doll on the ground again to activate the song and get a Max Ammo power-up.
