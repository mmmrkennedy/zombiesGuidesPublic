Wunderwaffe DG-2

The Wunderwaffe DG-2 can be Pack-a-Punched without completing an Easter Egg. It's also available through the mystery box and via the following quest:

  1. The Wunderwaffe requires four parts: the weapon barrel and three vacuum tubes. These can be obtained in any order:

  2. Weapon Barrel and First Vacuum Tube

  3. Navigate to the Fishing Hut and pick up the Weapon Barrel from a shelf near the door.
  4. Place the Weapon Barrel on the Radio Tower outside the Comm Room.
  5. At the start of the next round, a lightning bolt will strike the barrel and the Radio Tower will need to be defended.
  6. Once it's done, retrieve the Weapon Barrel from the Radio Tower and the charged Vacuum Tube from the radio in the 'Comm Room.'

  7. Second Vacuum Tube

  8. Pick up the Electrical Fuse from the table in the 'Comm Room.'
  9. Navigate to the Storage Hut and activate the trap. After a while, the trap will short out and the red box will pop open.
  10. Interact with the red box to insert the new fuse to start the trap again.
  11. Allow the trap to kill zombies until the tube on the workbench lights up.

  12. Third Vacuum Tube

  13. Wait for Zaballa to spawn on round 15.
  14. Lure her to the Tesla Coil in the Excavation Room/War Room.
  15. Bait her into attacking the Tesla Coil with her energy mines by standing near the coil.
  16. Once the Coil has been hit five times, pick up the vacuum tube from the table beside the coil.

  17. Crafting the Wunderwaffe DG-2

  18. Navigate to the Storage Hut and craft the Wunderwaffe DG-2 at the Workbench.

Main Easter Egg Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 3/10, Est. Completion Time - 25 to 40 Mins

  1. The Wunderwaffe DG-2 is required for the Easter Egg, but not until Step 9

    The Monolith & The Ceremony

  2. Navigate to 'Doctor's Quarter's Exterior' to find the Monolith covered in vines.
  3. Explode three Boom Schreiers next to the Monolith to clear the vines.
  4. Collect three Cypher Wheel parts:
    • Dormitory - On the table right of the M1 Garand wallbuy
    • Doctor's Quarters - In the room right of the Demonic Fountain
    • Dig Site - On a table in front of Pack-a-Punch
  5. Interact with the Monolith to place the Cypher Wheels on it.
  6. Three symbols need to be found and translated to be input into the Monolith. The locations are:
    • Excavation Room/War Room - On a table, the paper can be hidden beneath destructible books
    • Dig Site - On the workbench
    • Comm Room - On a table, left of the doorway to the other side of the building
  7. The symbols from Step 5 need to be translated. There are two ways to do this:
    • Using this graphic, translate the symbols from step 5 into symbols that can be input into the Monolith.
    • Inside the 'Doctor's Quarters,' throw a Molotov or thermite on this piece of paper to reveal all the symbols on the paper. Using this paper, translate the symbols.
  8. Input the translated symbols into the Monolith. If correct, a glowing stone will spawn near the edges of the area, one per player.
  9. Obtain the Wunderwaffe DG-2.
  10. All players must interact with a stone to "Start the Ceremony," which triggers a lockdown.
  11. During the lockdown, zombies shrouded in a blue mist will spawn that need to be killed near the shrine with the Wunderwaffe DG-2 before the lockdown ends.
  12. If completed successfully, a blue orb will spawn and a conversation will start. If failed, you can try again next round.

  13. Repairing The Mirror

  14. Go to the 'Flogger Courtyard' and fill the empty Demonic Fountain with blood by flinging zombies into it with the Flogger.
  15. Once filled enough, you can drink from the fountain to get the "Zombie Blood" effect (unlike previous maps, zombies can still see and attack you). It lasts 45 seconds.
  16. While in "Zombie Blood", check the map in the War Room for where the red 'X' is.
  17. Go to the hut indicated by the red "X" and look at the ceiling to find a mirror piece.
  18. Shoot the mirror piece to make it fall to the floor and pick it up.
  19. While in "Zombie Blood", a red orb will appear somewhere in the spawn building. Where the orb spawns determines its final destination. Orb Spawn/End Locations:
    • Start: 2nd Floor by the SVT-50 - End: Just left of the Doctor's Quarter's
    • Start: 1st Floor next to mystery box - End: In the back room of Fishing Hut
    • Start: 1st Floor by the storage door - End: In the middle of Dig Site
  20. Walk up to the orb to get it moving. You don't need to escort it after that.
  21. If you run out of "Zombie Blood" before grabbing the piece, drink from the fountain again and head to the orb's final destination.
  22. Once the orb reaches its destination, a mirror piece will spawn from it.
  23. Once both pieces are obtained, navigate to the 'Dig Site' and place the mirror on the central pedestal.
  24. Interact with the Blue Orb on a stone near the pedestal. Which pedestal your orb is on tells you which section the orb is flying to:
    • Pedestal closest to Combat shotgun --- Fishing Hut Area
    • Pedestal closest to PaP --- Comm Room Area
    • Pedestal to the far right of the PaP --- Doctor's Quarters Area
    • Pedestal the furthest North --- Storage Area
  25. Once you find the orb, shoot it to make it move. Repeat until the Orb returns to the 'Dig Site.'
  26. If you take too long to find/shoot the Orb, it respawns at the 'Dig Site' and you can interact with it to try again.

  27. Warning: Point of No Return. Once the Echo is summoned, the Boss Fight starts.

    Echo Boss Fight

  28. Tip: Equip the Energy Mine Field Upgrade at Tier 3 or higher — it nearly one-shots the Echo.
  29. All players must interact with the Blue Orbs in the 'Dig Site' to summon the Echo.
  30. After a short cutscene, the Echo will materialize as Saraxis and begin a conversation.
  31. Once the conversation ends, the Echo starts attacking. Kill the blue mist zombies near the mirror with the Wunderwaffe, same as during the Ceremony.
  32. Once enough Zombies are killed, a blue dome will appear over the area and the Echo will be weakened.
  33. To damage Echo, she must be inside the dome. Once she's in, shoot her in the mask before she recovers.
    • If you do enough damage, power-ups will spawn. If not, nothing spawns and you'll need to kill more zombies to weaken her again.
    • Damage doesn't carry over between phases. If you don't do enough while she's weakened, her health resets.
    • If equipped, use the Energy Mine Field Upgrade on the Echo now to instantly do enough damage and move to the next phase.
  34. Complete three phases, one per mask. Each phase requires killing a different zombie type to charge the mirror: blue mist Zombies, then Boom Schreiers, then Sturmkriegers.
  35. Once all the masks are destroyed, the Echo will be defeated and the repaired relic will be available to pick up.

Rampage Inducer

  1. To activate the Rampage Inducer, quickly shoot five lanterns hanging outside the map in the spawn room. A successful shot makes them flash orange:
    • Left of the SVT-50 wallbuy
    • Right of the SVT-50 wallbuy
    • Behind the Stairs
    • Left of the G-43 wallbuy, out the right side of the window
    • Left of the G-43 wallbuy, out the left side of the window
  2. If done correctly, a sound will play and the rampage inducer tablet will spawn.

Aether Bunny / Free Fire Sale

  1. Complete the Main Quest until Step 13 to be able to use "Zombie Blood".
  2. While in "Zombie Blood", collect three pieces of Mister Peeks (Aether Bunny) in the locations below:
    • Under the zipline platform
    • In the water outside the Fishing Hut
    • While the box is moving, where the box used to sit
  3. Once all parts are retrieved, 10,000 Bonus Points and a Fire Sale power-up will spawn nearby.
