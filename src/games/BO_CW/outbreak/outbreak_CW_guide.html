Wonder Weapons

The Ray Gun, D.I.E. and RAI-K 84 are available in Outbreak via the Loot Boxes, Bosses, Orda, Mystery Box, Trials, etc. Basically anyway you get loot.

There are no Easter Eggs to get these for free. You can get the D.I.E. Elemental Upgrades from specific location which are shown below.

    Electrobolt

  • The Electrobolt upgrade is on the large array on the right (when viewed on the map) in the Duga region.

    • Nova-5

  • The Nova-5 Upgrade is found on a Silo at the train loading area located near the western border of Golova.

    • Cryo-Emitter

  • The Cryo-Emitter upgrade is found in the middle of a frozen pond located by the Upper Highway near the southern border of the Alpine region.

    • Thermophasic

  • The Thermophasic upgrade is found in the Burnt Forest of the near the southern border of the Ruka region.

    Obtain the Beacon Listening Device

  1. Reach Round 3. After selecting warp at the beacon on round 1, shoot/melee the beacon till it breaks, and leans over to skip round 2 and go straight to round 3.
  2. You must find a radio (locations below) and interact with it. When you interact with it will start playing Feedback, and a bunch of zombies and mini-bosses will start spawning near you. Once you kill them, the subtitles will show a number. You must then find three amplifies located around the radio and set them to the same number as the main radio. Locations:

  3. Once the three Amplifiers are set to the correct number, go back to the main radio and interact with it. It will drop a Beacon Listening Device.
  4. Go to the Beacon and select the "Respond" option. If you don't want to listen to the dialogue, you can warp as soon as the beacon will let you interact with it again. Otherwise, warp to the next area when ever you'd like. If the next region is Ruka, you'll need to go to the next region after that.

    5. Obtain the Microfilm

  5. In the new region (if it's Ruka then go to the next region), there are four Stone Monkey Bomb Statues (Locations Below) you must find the one with an "M" drawn on a wall near it. When you find the Statue with the "M," you must shoot it, and it will drop Microfilm Reels. Shooting an incorrect statue won't fail you, so you could shoot them all until one drops the film.

  6. After Picking up the "Microfilm Reel," you must take it to a projector and interact with it three times. Each time a dialogue will play, and you must wait for it to finish before interacting with it again. Once the third dialogue starts, you can Warp using the Beacon.

  7. Once you spawn in Ruka, follow the mission objective to enter the Missile Silos. Once inside, navigate to the bottom floor of Silo A and locate the computer terminal labelled "Lift the Lockdown." Interact with it to start the cutscene.

    8. Finding and using the Launch Keys

  8. You must now find three launch keys. They're each tied to a series of steps and are located in Silo A, B and C respectively.

    9. Silo A: Monkey & Essence Trap

  9. On the bottom floor of Missile Silo A is a dead-end tunnel which contains an Essence Trap, collect it to replace your Tactical Equipment slot. (It is the same as the traps found in the Firebase Z Easter Egg)
  10. There will be an Aether Monkey in one of the vents along the walls of Silo A. You must slowly approach the vent the Monkey is in until it vanishes.
  11. Once it's gone, throw the Trap near the vent and then run away from the trap. Once you're far enough, the Monkey will teleport onto the trap, activate it to attempt to capture the Monkey.
  12. The Monkey will escape, but it will leave a key on the trap, interact with it to pick up the key.

    13. Silo B: Aetherium Canister & Jellyfish

  13. Interact with the Aetherium Harvesting Unit on the bottom floor of Missile Silo B.
  14. Next, locate and shoot the Aetherium Crystals, which line the walls of the silo to drop Aetherium Crystal Chunks.
  15. Once 20 chunks have been collected (shared inventory among all players), return to and interact with the Aetherium Harvesting Unit to deposit the Aetherium Crystal Chunks.
  16. Upon depositing all the required chunks, a wave of enemies will spawn. Eliminate these enemies, then interact with the harvesting unit to collect the Aetherium canister and replace your Field Upgrade.
  17. Navigate to Missile Silo D and locate the large Aether jellyfish. While standing on the staircase closest to the jellyfish, activate the Aetherium Field Upgrade to enter the jellyfish.
  18. While within the jellyfish, you're able to collect one of the launch keys.

    19. Silo C: Mimics

  19. In the Main Hub area connecting the silos, a bunch of mimics will spawn disguised as various items like TVs and Plants.
  20. After killing the Mimics, interact with the corpse that can be found within the corridor leading to Silo C.
  21. Interacting with the corpse will spawn Ruzarkon the Twisted. Defeat this HVT Mimic to collect one of the Launch Keys.

    22. Warning: Point of No Return. Once you activate the three missiles, you will be put into the Boss Fight

  22. Once all keys have been collected, you must activate all missiles in order within a time limit. Each missile can be activated using a control panel in the corresponding silo. If you activate them in the wrong order or run out of time, you can try again immediately.
  23. To find the correct order, you must look at the three lights below the keyhole on each control panel. The position of the green light will tell you where the silo is in the activation order.
    • For Example, If Silo D's Light is green in the third position and Silo A's Light is green in the second position, the activation order will be B, A, D .

    Legion Boss Fight

    Recommended: Death Machine Scorestreak

  24. Once you activate all three panels, a ten-minute countdown will start. You will have to complete the Boss Fight in this amount of time, or you will fail and have to start over.
  25. Go to the hub room (where all the mimics spawned) and run up the stairs to outside to start the boss fight.
  26. The Boss is a giant floating tempest. You must shoot it in the chest until it breaks and Legion becomes stunned. When stunned, three energy orbs appear. You must quickly try and destroy these orbs before Legion's stun wears off.
  27. Once all three orbs are destroyed, Legion will die, and you will have completed the Easter Egg.
  28. Tip: When one orb is destroyed, Legion will instantly retract the other Orbs no matter how long he has been stunned, so try to equally damage all the orbs without breaking any to maximize damage output while Legion is stunned. If you brought a Death Machine, don't use it on the Legion's chest as there is damage reduction instead use it on the Orbs.

'Operation Excision' Main Quest

Stats (Solo): Difficulty - 3/10, Est. Completion Time - 30 to 45 Mins

    Obtain the Beacon Listening Device

  1. Reach Round 3. After selecting warp at the beacon on round 1, shoot/melee the beacon till it breaks, and leans over to skip round 2 and go straight to round 3. If the round 3 region is the Sanatorium, you'll need to go to round 4 to get a non-sanatorium region.
  2. You must find a red rift and walk into it. You will be teleported into the sky, where you must fly into another rift located somewhere in the air in front of you. Repeat until a light comes flying out of the air with you and there are no more red rifts (about 3-4 rifts entered). Follow the light to where it lands on the ground to find the Beacon Listening Device.
    • If you miss a rift or take too long, it'll disappear, and you'll need to go to the next region to try again.
    Map Name Location On Map
    Alpine Map
    Armada Map
    Collateral Map
    Duga Map
    Golova Map
    Ruka Map
    Zoo Map
  3. Go to the Beacon and select the "Respond" option. If you don't want to listen to the dialogue, you can warp as soon as the beacon will let you interact with it again. Otherwise, warp to the next area whenever you'd like.
  4. Once you're in the Sanatorium region, find the crashed helicopter ( Location on Map ) and kill the enemies guarding it. Once the enemies are dead, interact with the Omega Audio Recording hanging out of the Helicopter.

    5. Assemble Modified Rover

  5. Locate and shoot the Red Aethereal Orb ( Locations on Map ) to get it to move to the Rover ( Location on Map ). The Orb moves in roughly the direction you shoot it, so position the orb between you and the Rover and shoot it. Repeat until it makes it to the Rover.
  6. Locate the Broken Mystery Box ( Location on Map ), which has an Aether Bunny sitting on top of it. Interact with the bunny to spawn a wave of enemies, eliminate these enemies, then interact with the bunny once more to equip it. You must bring it to the Rover.
  7. Once you place the Bunny in the Rover, the Orb will enter the Rover after a few seconds, and a radio will fall to the floor behind the Rover.

    8. Rover Escort / Orda Boss Fight

    Warning: Point of No Return. Once you activate Rover after putting the Aether Bunny in it, you will be put into the Boss Fight and you will lose access to PAP, Wunderfizz, etc.

  8. Activate the Rover and escort it until it reaches Monument Walk. Navigate to the rooftop of the Monument, as indicated by the waypoint, and interact with the Omega Audio Recording near the dead bodies.
  9. Once the recording is done, a 5:20 Countdown will start. You must defeat Orda and all the enemies before the timer runs out.
  10. Once all the enemies are defeated, board the Helicopter to start the cutscene and complete the Easter Egg.

'The Pact' Super Easter Egg (Spawn w/ upgraded weapon rarity)

  1. Complete at least two Easter Eggs on any maps.
  2. Warp to the 'Zoo' region either through completing objectives or via interacting with a mask in various regions. Interacting with a mask will make the next region you travel to be 'Zoo':
    • Alpine: At the base of a red fan in the 'Lower Chairlift' area --- Location on Map
    • Armada: In the 'Control Room' of the central ship --- Location on Map
    • Collateral: On a bucket beside a door to the 'Warehouse' --- Location on Map
    • Duga: At a bus stop in the 'Unused Lot' --- Location on Map
    • Golova: In front of the statue in the 'Town Center' --- Location on Map
    • Ruka: On a barrel in the middle of the 'Obstacle Course' --- Location on Map
    • Sanatorium: At the bottom of the 'Sanatorium Pool' --- Location on Map
  3. Navigate to this location in 'Zoo' and interact with the Ritual Site and watch the short cutscene.
  4. Based on the number of Easter Eggs completed, the starting weapon's rarity will change. Epic (purple) is all six quests completed, Rare (Blue) is four quests completed and Uncommon (Green) is two quests completed.
  5. Interacting with the Ritual Site again will disable the weapon upgrade, but you can come back when ever to get it back.
