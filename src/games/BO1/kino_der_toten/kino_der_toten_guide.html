Power

  • There is only one power switch and its on the stage .

Pack-a-Punch

  1. Turn on the Power.
  2. Interact with the teleporter mainframe on the stage to initiate the linking sequence. Then, interact with the teleporter pad in the spawn room to complete the link.
  3. Interact with the teleporter on the stage to teleport to the Pack-a-Punch room. All players inside the teleporter will be teleported.
  4. You have 30 seconds to Pack-a-Punch before you're forcefully teleported back. Any weapon in the machine when you teleport will be permanently lost. The seconds hand on the clock above the Pack-a-Punch machine will indicate how much time youâ€™ve left; you'll be teleporter when it hits 12.
  5. The teleporter will go on cooldown for 1 minute. You'll need to link the pad and the mainframe every time you want to teleport.

'115' Song (BO1, BO3)

Link to song on streaming here.

  • To start the song, interact with three hidden rocks around the map:
    • Spawn Room - In a jar near a Zombie barrier, "below" the stairs closest to Quick Revive
    • Dressing Room - On a stand by some mannequins, behind the MP5K/KN-44 wallbuy
    • Upstairs Alley Room - On a shelf with the letters, right of a zombie spawn

Film Reel (BO1, BO3)

  • Activate Pack-a-Punch.
  • As you're teleported out of the Pack-a-Punch projector room, you'll be brought to one of four random rooms. In one of these rooms, you'll see a film reel you can pick up.
  • With a film reel, interact with the Projector in the Pack-a-Punch room. It will change what's shown on the projector screen, and an audio log will start playing.
  • There are three total reels you can find and play.

Model Rocket Launch (BO1, BO3)

  1. Turn on the Power.
  2. In the Dressing Room, jump 5 times in front of the left one of these two mannequins near the door to the Speed Cola room.
  3. Teleport to/from Pack-a-Punch until you end up in the Pentagon-style room. Interact with the Rocket model to make it blast off.

'Samantha's Sorrow' Song + Free Max Ammo (BO3)

Link to song on streaming here.

  1. Stand beside the blue door in the Alley and count the number of knocks you hear. You will hear three distinct numbers with short pauses between them.
  2. Melee the door in the same pattern as you hear. Example, if you heard "115" then melee once, wait 1-2 seconds, then melee again, wait, then melee five times.
  3. Repeat this two more times with two different number sequences.
  4. If done correctly, you'll hear a sound, and the Samantha Doll will appear in front of the stage.
  5. Interact with the Doll to make it disappear. Five will spawn around the map, one at a time. The doll will make a sound when you're close to it. Shoot the doll to spawn the next one.
    • If you take too long to find a doll, Samantha will laugh. You can restart immediately by interacting with the first doll again.
  6. Doll Locations:
    1. Theatre Area - On a seat in a zombie spawn, on the Juggernog side
    2. Theatre Area - On top of a seat, right of Juggernog
    3. Theatre Area - On window sill of the Projector room
    4. Theatre Area - Hanging below the large "orb", below the main chandelier (above the turret)
    5. Theatre Area - On a seat in the first row on the right side
    6. Theatre Area - Down the stairs from the teleporter, in front of some canisters
    7. Theatre Area - On the broken part of the balcony above the initial doll location
    8. Theatre Area - On the light on the upper viewing area, left of the projector window
    9. Stage - On the metal platform with the ladder, above the door to the dressing room
    10. Stage - Behind the fake wall prop near the Wunderfizz
  8. Once you shoot the last doll, it'll spawn near the stage again, standing up. Interact with it to start the song and spawn a Max Ammo powerup.
