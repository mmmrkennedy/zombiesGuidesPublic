Bastard Sword

  1. Activate Pack-a-Punch.
  2. Starting on round 10, a Helmeted Zombie will spawn. This zombie is not part of the normal round progression (it can't be left as "last"), and its immune to headshot damage. Killing this zombie will drop a Stamp.

Caliburn (Fire)

  1. Get the Dragon Bastard Sword, then place it in front of the Dragon statue at the top of the stairs in Entrance Hall.
  2. 3 dots will light up on a stone map above the Dragon statue, indicating the locations of three cauldrons:
    • On the Hillside Ramparts, between Melee Macchiato and the Rampart Cannon
    • Next to the door between Upper Village (spawn) and Nature Path
    • In Upper Village (spawn)
  3. Interacting with a cauldron will set you on fire, dealing damage over time, but granting a speed boost. Go back to the Dragon statue and interact with it. Zombies will spawn along the way — killing them restores health.
  4. Once all 3 cauldrons' flames have been deposited in the Dragon statue, Caliburn will be available. Caliburn can be discarded by interacting with the Dragon Knight statue in the Dining Hall.

Durendal (Electricity)

  1. Get the Stag Bastard Sword.
  2. Obtain 3 Lightning Rods:
    • A zombie will spawn in the Dungeon once a player carrying the Stag Bastard Sword enters the area. Killing it drops a Lightning Rod.
    • Using a gun with the Dead Wire ammo mod, or a Shock Charge, hit a fuse box located on the right side of the staircase in Upper Village leading to the castle.
    • Pick it up next to the Deadshot Daiquiri machine in Hilltop.
  3. Go to Hillside Ramparts and place the rods inside a vase. This triggers a storm — survive inside it while holding the sword until lightning strikes and charges it. Once charged, hit the vase to charge one rod. Repeat two more times.
  4. Once all three rods are charged, place the sword into the vase to receive Durendal. Durendal can be discarded by interacting with the Stag Knight statue in the Dining Hall.

Balmung (Shadow)

  1. Get the Raven Bastard Sword.
  2. Pick up an Antiquity from the Alchemy Lab. The possible Antiquities are:
    • Lion Jaw (Panthera Leo)
    • Two-headed Crow Skull (Corvus Corone)
    • Ram/Urial Horn (Ovis Blanfordi)
    • Fish/Mudskipper Fossil (Oxudercinae)
    • Scorpion Fossil (Pandinus Imperator)
  3. Travel to the Tavern Cellar and interact with the circle device on the wall to place the sword and the Antiquity.
  4. Interact with the device and match the zodiac symbol and alchemical triangle at the bottom of the dial using the table below:
  5. Antiquity Zodiac Triangle
    Lion Jaw Leo ♌︎ 🜂 Fire
    2 Headed Bird Skull Gemini ♊︎ 🜁 Air
    Ram Horn Aries ♈︎ 🜂 Fire
    Fish Pisces ♓︎ 🜄 Water
    Scorpion Scorpio ♏︎ 🜄 Water
  6. The device will start ejecting void orbs that follow you. A portal will spawn on the floor of the Tavern Cellar — guide 3 orbs into it. The portal will then turn into an orb and move to the Tavern, turning back into a portal. Repeat the process. The orb will move once more in the Tavern next to the pool table. Once the final 3 void orbs have entered the portal, it will travel back to the dial in the Tavern Cellar.
  7. Interact with the dial. The Raven Bastard Sword will be swallowed into a void portal and transformed into Balmung. Balmung can be discarded by interacting with the Raven Knight statue in the Dining Hall.

Solais (Light)

  1. Get the Lion Bastard Sword.
  2. Glowing Parasites will start to spawn. Kill one Parasite to charge the sword, then hit the door with a lion knocker at the bottom of the stairs in Upper Village. This lights up a symbol. Repeat the process three more times.
  3. Interact with the door to teleport into a corridor.
  4. Insert the sword into the pedestal at the end of the corridor. Four Alchemical symbols (☉ 🜔 🜍 🜹) will float in the air, each shining in front of the pedestal one at a time starting from the top, showing the order they need to be shot.
  5. Once the four symbols are shot in the correct order, Solais will be available to pick up and you will be teleported back into the map. Solais can be discarded by interacting with the Lion Knight statue in the Dining Hall.

Incantations / Rituals

The Incantations can only be obtained after speaking to Krafft in the Dungeon.

Fire Incantation (Ra's Ankh)

  1. Light 3 bonfires around the Hillside Ramparts:
    • Above the Elemental Pop room
    • Above the entrance to the Castle
    • Above Speed Cola
  2. Once all 3 bonfires are lit, a Doppelghast will spawn. Killing it will drop the Ra's Ankh.
  3. Bring the Ra's Ankh to the entrance of the Courtyard from Speed Cola, place it there, and fill it with souls.
  4. Once filled, a chest will spawn. Melee this chest with Caliburn to open it, revealing the Fire Incantation.

Dark Incantation (Raven's Talon)

  1. Take the Cave Slide from the Oubliette to the Town Square. Near the end, before the sewer section, there will be a Raven on the right. Shoot the Raven. You can also do this from the exit of the Cave Slide using an explosive.
  2. The Raven will now be flying above the Town Square. Shoot it again and it will drop the Raven's Talon.
  3. Bring the Raven's Talon to the Undercroft, place it to the right of the Mystery Box, and fill it with souls.
  4. Once filled, a chest will spawn. Melee this chest with Balmung to open it, revealing the Dark Incantation.

Light Incantation (Paladin's Brooch)

  1. Shoot the red crystal above the Vulture Aid perk machine; this will cause a red beam of light to reflect off the crystal. Continue this process until the beam is solid and reflecting off every crystal.
  2. The crystals will reveal the Paladin's Brooch resting on the bust in the Alchemical Lab.
  3. Bring the Paladin's Brooch to the lion graffiti in the Dining Hall, place it there, and fill it with souls.
  4. Once filled, a chest will spawn. Melee this chest with Solais to open it, revealing the Light Incantation.

Electric Incantation (Pegasus' Horseshoe)

  1. Shoot Otto's Horseshoe down in the Hilltop Barn and pick it up.
  2. Bring the horseshoe to the Rampart Cannon and launch yourself out of it.
  3. Find where the horseshoe landed (there will be lightning clouds nearby) — either at the Hilltop or Village Square — and pick up the Pegasus' Horseshoe.
  4. Bring the Pegasus Horseshoe to the Town Square, place it to the left of the statue, and fill it with souls.
  5. Once filled, a chest will spawn. Melee this chest with Durendal to open it, revealing the Electric Incantation.

Main Quest

  1. Setup

  2. Speak to Gabriel Krafft by interacting with a door in the Dungeon.
  3. Grab the 4 Torn Pages from the Sitting Rooms. Each page will always spawn in the same general area of the room. If you want to skip some of the following steps, note which pages have which eye symbols without picking them up and skip to Step 5. Note that the 4th page will always correspond to the Point of Power trap in the Sitting Rooms.
    • Page I (Top Left) - Against the walls and boxes near the door leading to Entrance Hall
    • Page II (Bottom Left) - Around the bunk beds
    • Page III (Top Right) - Around the TV stand/sofa and TVs next to Stamin-Up
    • Page IV (Bottom Right) - Against the corridor wall between Alchemical Lab and the Oubliette

  4. Alchemical Code Wall

  5. Melee the off-colored square on the wall in the Undercroft with a Melee Macchiato punch to reveal a grid of alchemical symbols that switch places every few seconds.
  6. You need to enter a 6-symbol code by shooting the symbols in the correct order. Go to the Tavern and find 6 dark green flasks labeled "I" through "VI". Interact with each flask to reveal a blue alchemical symbol from the grid. Return to the Undercroft and shoot the corresponding symbols — be careful not to shoot too fast, as the symbols change position rapidly.
  7. If done correctly, an orb will open parts of the wall to the left of the grid, revealing a book with missing pages. Interact with the book to place the 4 torn pages.

  8. Point of Power Traps

  9. (Step 5) Each Torn Page shows a specific Point of Power trap on the map. Following the page's numerical order, head to each indicated trap. Activate the trap and get about 10 kills. If done correctly, a quote will play and the trap will immediately shut down, and the corresponding symbol will turn grey confirming the step was done. The final trap will always be located in the Sitting Rooms. Once all traps are done, an orb holder with four symbols will spawn on the wall between the Sitting Rooms and Oubliette.

  10. Knight Salutes

  11. Obtain all 4 Elemental Swords and all 4 Incantations.
  12. Examine the code around the orb holder. The code uses the symbols of each Bastard Knight (Dragon, Stag, Raven, Lion) marked I–IV clockwise. This determines the order in which you must cast the corresponding Incantation in front of each statue in the Dining Hall while holding the respective Elemental Sword. Quickly start the sword inspect animation while facing the knight — the animation does not need to complete. If done correctly, the knight will salute and glow. Repeat for each knight in the displayed order.
  13. Knight Incantation Sword
    Stag Electric Durendal
    Raven Dark Balmung
    Lion Light Solais
    Dragon Fire Caliburn

  14. Elemental Challenges

  15. Once all statues are done, a white Mystic Orb will appear above the room. Bring the orb to the holders near each incantation ritual site and insert it to start the elemental challenge. Once completed, a small colored energy orb will orbit the Mystic Orb.
    • Fire: Bring the orb to the holder next to the trap in Courtyard. Get kills by burning Zombies with Caliburn, Molotovs, Fire Incantation, or Napalm Burst ammo mod.
    • Void: Bring the orb to the holder next to the Dark Incantation ritual in the Undercroft. Get kills with Balmung's charged attack, Void Incantation, or Shadow Rift ammo mod.
    • Electricity: Bring the orb to the holder against the gate next to the Light Incantation ritual in Town Square. Get kills with Durendal's charged attack, Electric Incantation, or Dead Wire ammo mod.
    • Light: Bring the orb to the holder located above the gate in Dining Hall, opposite of Vulture Aid. Green orbs will appear — run across each one around the Dining Hall as fast as possible.

  16. Warning: Point of No Return. Depositing the Mystic Orb into the final wall will trigger the Boss Fight.

  17. Boss Fight

  18. Once all 4 elemental challenges are completed, place the Mystic Orb in the wall between the Sitting Rooms and the Oubliette. The wall will open to reveal a hidden room — the Secret Study. Interact with the Ancient Mystic Recording on the stone table. Pick up the Guardian Key from the table and bring it to the statue in the Town Square. At the statue's feet, deposit the key to activate and enter the boss fight.

Rat Crown (Free Loot / Crown Cosmetic)

  1. Obtain a cheese wheel from a display window in the Town Square.
  2. Go up to 10 rats around the map and stare at them to scare them away. Rat locations:
    • Town Square - Under a flower bed
    • Village Ascent - Under a van
    • Entrance Hall - Under a chair
    • Next to the Rampart Cannon - Under stairs
    • Dining Hall - Under a wooden pallet
    • Dungeon - Inside a cell
    • Alchemical Lab - Under the stone showcasing elements
    • Sitting Rooms - Under a table near the bunk beds
    • Near Deadshot Daiquiri - In front of a zombie spawn hole
    • Undercroft - Next to a Mystery Box location
  3. Bring the cheese wheel to a plate in the Undercroft. The rats will eat the cheese and then leave.

Having at least 1 rat eat the cheese will drop various loot such as salvage, ammo mods, and weapon upgrade materials. If all 10 rats eat the cheese, a golden crown will also drop, which your character will wear for the remainder of the match once picked up. Only 1 player per match can wear the crown.

Chess Guardian (Temporary Ally)

  1. Find a white knight chess piece in one of 3 locations:
    • Under the Tavern on a shelf
    • On a table in the Dining Hall Passage
    • On a table across from Elemental Pop
  2. Bring the chess piece to the chess board in the Sitting Rooms. Interact with the chessboard and an aethereal field will surround the table. Kill zombies within the field to fill it with souls.

Once enough souls are deposited, the Chess Guardian will spawn and act as a protector for a limited number of rounds. The Guardian will only kill zombies, not revive downed players.

Bartending (Free PHD Flopper)

  1. Pick up 3 bottles of liquor around the map:
    • Under a table in the Undercroft
    • Next to a couch in the Dining Hall
    • On a shelf in the Sitting Rooms
  2. Go to the Tavern and interact with the drawers below the blue liquor to start the bartending mini-game. You may need to crouch to interact.
  3. Play the bartending mini-game by interacting with liquor glasses at the bar and placing them in front of zombies that approach. The zombies will glow to indicate the color of liquor they want. Successful bartending will earn you points as tips. After bartending long enough, participating players will be granted PHD Flopper.

If you have a glass of the wrong color, interact with the trash can behind the bar to discard it.

Mr. Peeks (Free Perk)

  1. In the room with Elemental Pop in the Hillside Ramparts, look through the window to find Mr. Peeks dancing above a blue orb. Shoot the orb with a bullet weapon — Mr. Peeks will laugh and disappear.
  2. In the Barn in the Nature Path, look through a hole in the Barn towards the castle to find and shoot another Mr. Peeks dancing above a blue orb.
  3. From the 2nd floor of the Dining Hall, stand next to a flood light and look upwards at a window. Find and shoot Mr. Peeks' orb again.
  4. Inside the Dungeon, Mr. Peeks will be sitting in a jail cell on a bed next to the Ammo Crate. Shoot the final orb and you will receive a free perk.

Maya's Revenge (Free Legendary GS45)

  1. Start a match playing as the Maya operator.
  2. Starting on round 12, go up to this door and interact with the radio.
  3. The Maya player can enter a wave defense in a hallway. Survive against zombies and Doppleghasts.
  4. Once the wave defense is done, interact with a door in the hallway to watch a story cutscene. Afterwards, the Maya player will appear outside the first door, and a legendary-tier GS45 will be on the table next to the radio.

Alchemical Incantation Defense (Free Loot)

  1. Complete Step 4 of the Main Quest (the Alchemical Code Wall).
  2. Once all of the symbols on the Alchemical code wall have dimmed, use one of each Incantation on the wall to light up a row of symbols.
  3. Once all symbols are active again, a lockdown defense will initiate in the room. Survive 3 waves of zombies.

Once the lockdown is complete, various loot will appear in the room for you to pick up.

Fireplace Wisps (Temporary Zombie Killers)

Throw Molotovs at the following fireplaces in rapid succession. Failing to do so within the time limit will cause the fires to go out.

  • Dining Hall
  • Alchemical Lab
  • Sitting Rooms
  • Tavern

Once all 4 are lit in succession, flaming wisps will fly out of the fireplaces, killing zombies they encounter. This can be repeated multiple times per game.

Free Power-Ups

  • Around Citadelle des Morts, there are statues of various power-ups that spawn the corresponding power-up when shot. Locations:
    • Max Ammo: Window-sill at Upper Village
    • Full Power: Roof of Dining Hall
    • Insta-Kill: On scaffolding above the Tavern (above the exit of the Cave Slide)
    • Max Armor: Inside the Entrance Hall, on the roof
    • Nuke: Above Elemental Pop room at the Hillside Ramparts. You may need an explosive to reach it.
    • Bonus Points: Behind a couch in the Undercroft
    • Double Points: Inside a zombie spawn window in the Alchemical Lab

Wishing Well Bank (Free Points)

  • Throw Frag Grenades into the well in the Village Square during a Pest round to cause it to spew point drops. You can then interact with the well and deposit points into it. The points can be reclaimed by throwing another Frag into it. This works with Double Points, potentially doubling the points you pick up.
    • Note: The exact number of grenades required is unconfirmed.

Pool Table (Free 100 Points)

  • Shoot any of the balls on the Pool Table in the Tavern. Every ball will fall into a pocket and 100 points will spawn.
